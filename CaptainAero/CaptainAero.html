Captain Aero's first adventure was written by Allen Ulmer and illustrated by Ray Willner. Captain Aero was a Flying ace for the US Army. In his first adventure he tested out an experimental P-60 plane and escorted a flight of Canadian flyers taking planes to England as America and not yet entered World War II. He also had his own fan club The Sky Scouts advertised on the front cover.
