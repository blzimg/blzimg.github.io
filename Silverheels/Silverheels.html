Silverheels


Series: 3 issue mini series by PC. 4th issue is Graphic Novel by Eclipse.
Publisher: 1983 PC

Stories by Bruce Jones and April Campbell. Art by Scott Hampton and Ken Steacy. Cover by Scott Hampton. A science fiction miniseries from influential independent publisher Pacific Comics. Silverheels lives in a future reservation, but he dreams of life on the outside. But escape reveals a world under the thumb of an alien empire, where a fascistic state struggles to maintain its hold on power. Also featuring a Flan backup story. Silverheels; Robotus Ridiculous.

Silverheels escapes from the reservation and joins the aliens cadet training program. Also featuring a Flan backup story and two 5-page pinup galleries, one for Scott Hampton and one for Ken Steacy. Silverheels; War of the Blorts!

A chance encounter with an aristocrats daughter named Miranda gives Silverheels some extra incentive, as he faces the aliens toughest training regimen yet. Also featuring a Flan backup story, and an Aliso Road story by Jaime Hernandez of Los Bros Hernandez. Silverheels; Flan, Youre Fired!; All My Love, Aliso Road. Intended as a four-issue miniseries, but this was the final issue; the saga was concluded in Silverheels GN (1987 Eclipse) #1-1ST.
