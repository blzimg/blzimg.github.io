Fifth Beatle: The Brian Epstein Story


Series: 2013 Graphic Novel
Publisher: Dark Horse

Written by Vivek J. Tiwary
Illustrations by Andrew Robinson and Kyle Baker

The Fifth Beatle is the untold true story of Brian Epstein, the visionary manager who discovered and guided the Beatles to unprecedented international stardom, yet died painfully lonely at the young age of thirty-two.

More than merely the story of "The Man Who Made the Beatles," The Fifth Beatle is an uplifting, tragic, and ultimately inspirational human story about the desire to leave behind a legacy, amidst the struggle to overcome crushing personal obstacles and insurmountable odds!
