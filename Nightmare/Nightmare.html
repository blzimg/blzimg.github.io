Series: 4 issues 1953 (#10 - 13)
Publisher: St. John

First issue in the series starts with issue #10 (continued from Weird Horrors #9). Reprints Weird Thrillers (Ziff-Davis) with new Joe Kubert cover and two pages Everett Raymond Kinstler art. "The Fisherman of Space" (art by Alex Toth), "The Last Man," "the Cycle of Time" (art by Murphy Anderson), "Murderer's Mask" (art by Gene Colan).
