2099 Sketchbook


Series: one-shot
Illustrator: cover by Rick Leonardi
Writer: Joey Cavalieri
Publisher: 1993 by Marvel

About: 2099 Sketchbook features drafting table sketches of 2099 Unlimited, Doom 2099, Punisher 2099, Ravage 2099, Spider-Man 2099, and X-Men 2099, with commentary by 2099 editor Joey Cavalieri.
