Dez Skinn's Quality Periodicals took over the repackaging of IPC Magazines Ltd material for the North American market from Nick Landau's Eagle Comics in 1986. When IPC sold its comics division to Robert Maxwell and Fleetway Publications in 1987, responsibility for producing these reprints was transferred to S.Q.P. Inc., which continued using the Quality label for a time before switching to the Fleetway Quality label.
