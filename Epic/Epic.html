Series: 4 issue mini-series 1992 by Marvel imprint Epic

A collection of new stories continuing some of Epic's most popular series to celebrate the Epic line's tenth anniversary! Including the following - Clive Barker's Hellraiser: Birth Rite by D.G. Chichester and William Simpson; Stalkers (4 Parts) by Mark Verheiden and D'Israeli; Dreadlands by Andy Lanning, Steve White, Phil Gascoine and Kev Hopgood; Wild Cards (4 Parts) by Lewis Shiner, Walter Jon Williams, Mike Collins and Kev Hopgood. Covers by Dave McKean.
