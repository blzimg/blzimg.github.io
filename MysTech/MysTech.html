Series: 4 issues 1993
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Dan Abnett
Illustrations by Bryan Hitch, Jeff Anderson

X-Men, Avengers. The MyS-TECH immortals' plans for the UnEarth come to fruition, while demons are sent to deal with the X-Men and the Avengers in "Strange Screams of Death." Nick Fury, Dark Angel, Wolverine, Captain America, Doctor Strange, X-Factor, Albion, Union Jack, Warheads, Psycho Warriors, Fantastic Four, Death's Head II.

Crowe captures Nick Fury and tortures him. Earth's heroes are put on alert by huge power surges as the Mys-Tech council begins to merge un-Earth with the real world.
