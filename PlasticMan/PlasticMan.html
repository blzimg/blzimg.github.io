Plastic Man


Creator: Jack Cole
Series: on-going
Publisher: 1943 Quality Comics

Plastic Man (real name Patrick "Eel" O'Brian) is a fictional character originally published by Quality Comics and later acquired by DC Comics. Created by cartoonist Jack Cole, Plastic Man was one of the first superheroes to incorporate humor into mainstream action storytelling. The character hasThe first appearance was in Police Comics #1 in 1941. He has been published in several solo series and has interacted with other characters in the mainstream DC Universe as a member of the Justice League. He has additionally appeared in several television and video game adaptations, including a short-lived television show of his own.
