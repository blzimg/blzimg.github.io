Genetix

Series: 6 issue mini series, codename 4 issue mini series
Publisher: 1993 Marvel

Genetix #1: A 6-issue limited series about a group of MyS-TECH operatives who break away from the villainous group. In order to find out about their real pasts, they break through the false memories that MyS-TECH programmed into their brains. Death's Head II appearance. Written by Graham Marks and Andy Lanning. Art and wraparound cover by Phil Gascoine and Robin Riggs.

Codename #1: A 4 issue mini series. Battle under London! Introducing a group of five genetically enhanced teenagers whose first mission will take them into the Savage Land with Wolverine! Written by Andy Lanning and Graham Marks.
