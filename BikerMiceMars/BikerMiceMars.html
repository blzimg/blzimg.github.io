Biker Mice from Mars


Series: 3 issue mini series cartoon adaptation
Illustrator: Rurick Tyler
Writer: Rick Ungar, Bob Forward
Publisher: 1993 Marvel

Starring Throttle, Modo, Vinnie, Greasepit, and Lawrence Limburger. A trio of motorcycle-riding rodents battles aliens with designs on Earth. Based on the science fiction/action animated series created by Rick Ungar.
