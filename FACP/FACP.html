the Further Adventures of Cyclops and Phoenix


Series: 4 issue mini-series 1996, 1 Collection (TPB) 1997
Publisher: Marvel

Presenting the story that revealed the epic origin of the man called Sinister! Hurled 150 years into the past, Cyclops and Phoenix encounter Nathaniel Essex - the brilliant geneticist who they know is destined to become Mr. Sinister! Can they save his soul and defeat the newly-risen Apocalypse?
