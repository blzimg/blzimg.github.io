Justice

Series: 52 issues 1947
Publisher: Atlas (Marvel)

Continued from Wacky Duck, Justice ran during 1947 through 1955 for 44 issues. This series featured crime-themed adventures, in which justice always prevailed. It was continued as Tales Of Justice with issue 53.

The title would have head lines like "FBI in Action", "Law in Action", "Every Case Taken From Real Life", "True Cases Proving Crime Can't Win", and "It's Thrilling Because It's True!".
