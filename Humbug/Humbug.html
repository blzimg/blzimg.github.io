11 issues from 1957-1958 by Humbug Publishing, edited by Harvey Kurtzman. Several of the project's contributing artists had previously worked with Kurtzman when he was the editor of Mad, including Wallace Wood, Jack Davis, Al Jaffee and Will Elder. The 32-page first issue (August 1957) featured a front cover by Will Elder with the announcement "The End of the World Is Coming", only ten billion years left. While waiting, read Humbug for laughs.
