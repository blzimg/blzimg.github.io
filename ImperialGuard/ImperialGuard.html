Imperial Guard

Series: 3 issues 1997
Publisher: Marvel

Cover pencils by Chuck Wojtkiewicz, inks by Ray Snyder. Script by Brian Augustyn, pencils by Chuck Wojtkiewicz, inks by Ray Snyder.

"Imperious Wrecks!"
In the aftermath of Onslaught, the Imperial Guard are left on Earth to observe and help out since many heroes have disappeared. The Guard find themselves up against a militia group who has it out for super beings but seem to have some pretty super powers of their own. Uncanny X-Men #341 takes place midway through this issue.
