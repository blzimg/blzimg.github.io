Every comic book publisher tried to capitalize on the success of EC Comics’ Mad with varying success. Eh! was Charlton’s entry into the satire and parody comics arena. The full title is Eh! Dig This Crazy Little Mixed-Up Mag as a play on the lengthy Tales Calculated to Drive You Mad. Unfortunately, to have the success of Mad, you had to have writing that approaches the level of Mad. Eh! did not and soon went by the wayside of other short-lived comics like Flip, Bughouse, Whack, and Wild.
