Great Lakes Avengers


Series: 4 issues 2005
Publisher: Marvel

Written by Dan Slott Pencils & Cover by Paul Pelletier
They are the Great Lake Avengers, the guys who got the shortest end of the super-hero stick. But with the REAL Avengers disassembled, they're going to try to step up to the plate and deal with one of their most powerful villains! Can they save the day? Well, we just hope these guys can save themselves! Special appearances by: Captain America, Thor, the Vision, the Scarlet Witch, and Hawkeye.
