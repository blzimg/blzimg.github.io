Adventures of the Mask


Series: Adventures of is an off shoot of The Mask
Illustrator: Jay Geldhof, Marc Campos
Writer: Michael Eury
Publisher: 1996 Dark Horse

About: Comic book adaptation of the TV cartoon based on the 1994 film starring Jim Carrey which was based on the comic series The Mask. Apparently it sold well enough that an Omnibus collection was created as well as a special Toys R Us issue. That's show business folks, go figure.

Story: A mild man turns into a crime fighter with special powers when wearing an ancient mask.

Toys R Us Edition: Pretorius dresses up as the Mask for Halloween to try to give the Mask a bad reputation.
