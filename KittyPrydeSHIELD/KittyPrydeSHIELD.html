Kitty Pryde Agent of SHIELD

Series: 3 issue mini series 1997
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Larry Hama
Illustrations by Jesus Redondo and Sergio Melia

When S.H.I.E.L.D.'s computers go haywire and spit out information concerning Kitty Pryde, G.W. Bridge leads the covert agency straight to Muir Island to get answers. Despite Pete Wisdom's wishes, Kitty escorts the suave spy named Rigby onboard the massive Helicarrier, but before the computer hack can get the system on-line, the fiend known as Ogun strikes! His goal: possess the soul of his former student! His next target: Wolverine!
