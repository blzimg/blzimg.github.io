Ape Entertainment is an American independent comic book company founded in 2003. The brainchild of comics devotees Brent E. Erwin, Mike Hall, and David Hedgecock. They have some popular titles like Go-Go Gorilla & The Jungle Crew, Sullengrey, Horrorwood, and even the home for Shrek Comics, published under their all ages comic line KiZoic.
