3 issues in 1998 by Marvel

A short-lived anthology title from Marvel featuring black and white stories and articles.

Issue #1:

  • Cover by Brian Stelfreeze.
  • Punisher illustration by Rodolfo Damaggio.
  • Short articles on Gene Ha and Gerard Jones.
  • "Free Fall," script by Gerard Jones, art by Gene Ha. Origin of Black Widow. Widow fights a Soviet Super Soldier who turns out to be her older brother.
  • Short article on Bernie Wrightson. "Limited Intellect!" starring Hulk, script and art by Bernie Wrightson.
  • Short articles on Len Wein and Steve Ditko.
  • "A Man's Reach...!" starring Iron Man, script by Steve Ditko (plot) and Len Wein, art by Steve Ditko.
  • Short articles on Ron Marz and Brian Stelfreeze.
  • Frankenstein vs. Wolfman illustration by Tom Grindberg.
  • Thor illustration by Cam Smith.
  • Wolverine illustration by Joe Bennett.
