Astonishing Ant-Man


Series: on-going
Illustrator: Ramon Rosanas, Mark Brooks (cover)
Writer: Nick Spencer
Publisher: 2015 Marvel

Scott Lang was a down-on-his-luck, divorced ex-con, until he stole renowned scientist and super hero Hank Pym's size-altering technology in the hopes of saving his daughter's life. Through that act of doing wrong for the right reasons, Scott inadvertently learned how to be a hero himself.

With Pym's blessing and his daughter Cassie at his side, he became... The Astonishing Ant-Man! But when a curveball upends Scott's life, will he continue to be a hero, or will he return to his former life of crime?
