Ender's Shadow


Series: 5 issue mini-series and 1 collection (TPB) 2008
Publisher: Marvel

Creative Director ORSON SCOTT CARD. Written by MIKE CAREY. Art by SEBASTIAN FIUMARA. Cover by PASQUAL FERRY.

A mysterious orphan on the streets of Rotterdam, no bigger than a bean, will change the world. There's only one student at Battle School smarter than Ender Wiggin-Bean.
