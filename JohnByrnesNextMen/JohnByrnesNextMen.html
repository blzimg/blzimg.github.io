John Byrne's Next Men

Series: 31 issues (#0-30) 1992
Publisher: Dark Horse
Written by John Byrne
Illustrations by John Byrne

Collecting the four stories previously published in Dark Horse Presents, Bethany, Nathan, Danny, Jack, and Jasmine suddenly find themselves awake in a world completely foreign to their memories. They remember having strange abilities, but nothing like this! What are they? They're part of the mystery that Tony Murcheson has been assigned to investigate and uncover: Project Next Men!
