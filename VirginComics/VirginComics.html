Virgin Comics is an Indian comic book Publishers company, founded in 2006, which produced stories for an international audience. The company was founded by Sir Richard Branson and his Virgin Group, author Deepak Chopra, filmmaker Shekhar Kapur, and entrepreneurs Sharad Devarajan, Suresh Seetharaman, and Gotham Chopra. In August 2008, the company restructured and relocated from New York to Los Angeles and renamed Liquid Comics, then renamed to Graphic India in 2011. Website Graphicindia.com
