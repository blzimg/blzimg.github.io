Series: 12 issue mini series 1988
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Roy Thomas
Illustrations by Rich Buckler

The beginning of a series recapping the history of Namor, the Sub-Mariner. This issue covers the ancient origin of undersea Atlantis, the rise of Homo Mermanus, Kamuu's empire, Lemuria and the Serpent Crown, and the relocation to Antarctica, all leading up to 1920 and the meeting of Princess Fen and Leonard McKenzie that results in the birth of Prince Namor and tells the story of his childhood.
