Series: 2 issues 1996
Written by Manny Coto
Illustrated by John Stokes
Cover by Geof Darrow
Publisher: Dark Horse

The peaceful planet of Trelkas is invited to become part of a distant alliance of worlds. Some of the Trelkin leaders are cautious, but the Consensus -- their version of parliament or senate -- makes the mistake of sending an envoy to the alliance.

Written by Manny Coto. Art by John Stokes. Cover by Geof Darrow. The battle grows on the planet Trelkas as the peace-loving natives try to defend themselves against the invading Broodwarriors. Ignored for his own aggressiveness before the war, scientist Tenris now holds the only hope of defense for the dying world.
