Damage Control


Series: 4 issue mini series
Publisher: 1989 Marvel

"A Restoration Comedy!" Story by Dwayne McDuffie. Art by Ernie Colon and Bob Wiacek.

Ever wonder who cleans up the epic mess after the super heroes vanquish the giant robot that has destroyed half of downtown New York City? Well, wonder no more. Their name is Damage Control, and their business is restoration, on a super level. Featuring appearances by the Avengers (Thor, She-Hulk, and Black Knight), Spider-Man, and Thunderball. Includes Official Handbook dossiers for Damage Control employees John Porter and Lenny.
