Flight of Angels


Series: 2011 Graphic Novel
Publisher: Vertigo (DC)

Created, Illustrated by Rebecca Guay (Magic: The Gathering, Veils)
Written by Bill Willingham (FABLES) and Holly Black (The Spiderwick Chronicles)

ANGELS: Guardians. Messengers. Warriors. Fallen. All these angelic aspects and more are explored in A FLIGHT OF ANGELS, a riveting tale in the tradition of The Decameron and The Canterbury Tales.

A mysterious angel plummets to Earth and lands deep in a dark forest, where his dying body is found by the mystical denizens of this strange place. As the gathered fauns, fairies, hags and hobgoblins debate what to do with him, each tells a different story of who they imagine this celestial creature to be: a hero, a lover, a protector or a killer.
