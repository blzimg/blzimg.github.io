3 issues in 1996 by Marvel

Script by Paul Grist, pencils by Karl Kerschl, inks by Greg Adams.

Ben Urich discovers that Morgan Hall is trying to form a crime syndicate and flees after being discovered. Ben and Angela Yin are chased by thugs for their pictures of the events. They discover that they've photographed Congressman Henry Adams cavorting with Susie Dawn, and Hall wants the pictures so he can blackmail Adams. The Daily Bugle runs the story and Adams confesses. Peter Parker saves Betty from being shot and they witness the murder of Jimmy Fude.

How far is J. Jonah Jameson willing to distort the truth to protect the reputation of a friend and his own? Plus, will Betty Brant's foray into the underworld yield potentially fatal fruit?

What happens when the reporters become the news? While the staff of the Bugle redouble their efforts to locate the abducted Betty Brant, Betty herself uncovers the connections between Tommy Fude and mobster Morgan Hall, a revelation that may cost her her life!
