Series: 5 issues 1954
Publisher: Atlas

Mad Magazing rip off parodies of Charlie Chan, Rip Van Winkle, and Dr. Jekyl/Mr. Hyde: "Charlie Chin Meets Sleek Wily" (art by Joe Maneely), "Rip Wan Stinkle" (art by Ed Win), "Dr. Jackal and Mr. Hide" (art by Bill Everett), "The Frozen North," "Captain O.U. Kidd" (art by Al Hartley), and a two-page article on Don Quixote, "Don Chaotic." Maneely cover.
