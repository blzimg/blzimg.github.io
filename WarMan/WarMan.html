Series: 2 issues 1993
Written by Chuck Dixon
Art by Juan Zanotto
Cover by Liam Sharp
Publisher: Epic

Arms dealer Griff makes war his job but none of his business! That is, until he's an innocent bystander during an assassination attempt and shot down with his own product. Will Griff develop a conscience? You'll be surprised at the answer. This Heavy Hitter includes a bound-in trading card.

Hard-hitting jungle combat in the conclusion of this Heavy Hitter, complete with a bound-in trading card. Griffin and his two men are being tracked by the mercenaries who shot them down in deep jungle. But who'll get Griffin first, the mercs or the treacherous jungle itself?
