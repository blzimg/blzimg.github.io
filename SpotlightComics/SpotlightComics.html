Series: 3 issues 1944
Publisher: Chesler

Action anthology featuring Black Dwarf. Other characters included Barry Kuda, Steve "Globe" Trotter, Veiled Avenger, Sgt. Steele.

Issue #1 cover of Black Dwarf by George Tuska. Issue #2 and Issue #3 cover of Black Dwarf by Paul Gattuso.

Murder Mystery and high tension suspense weave across the city as the Black Dwarf and his strange band of ex-crooks use their criminal experience and methods on a case that baffles the police.

Barry Kuda was a water-breathing adventurer native to Merma, an underwater kingdom. He defended the kingdom from its many hostile neighbors, He was assisted by his sidekick Algie.While he has no superpowers other than being able to breath under water, he did know how to use a number of old fashion weapons such as maces, spears, and swords.
