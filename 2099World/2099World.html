Illustrated by Pasqual Ferry, written by Ben Raab and Joe Kelly, 8 issues from 1996-1997 by Marvel.

With the imminent cancellation of the 2099 sub-imprint, Marvel gathered many of the characters from that continuity into this umbrella title. X-Men 2099, Spider-Man 2099, Doom 2099, Doctor Strange, and a myriad of original characters struggle to achieve their collective goals and win some time in the spotlight in the world at the end of the next century.
