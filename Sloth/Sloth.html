Series: Graphic Novel 2006
Publisher: Vertigo

Sloth is a graphic novel by American cartoonist Gilbert Hernandez, published by Vertigo (DC Comics) in 2006.

The story opens with the teenaged Miguel Serra awakening from a year-long coma. The surreal tale unravels as its protagonists delve into the legends of the sleepy suburban town they live in. The book is unrelated to Hernandez's Palomar stories.
