Astonishing Thor


Series: on-going
Illustrator: Esad Ribic, Mike Choi
Writer: Rob Rodi
Publisher: 2011 Marvel

Waves are but water, wind but air. And though lightning be fire... IT MUST ANSWER THUNDER'S CALL. The God of Thunder finds himself battling a mysterious surge of natural catastrophes, hurricanes, tidal waves, earthquakes. At first, the Son of Odin suspects Zephyr, immortal mistress of the winds, but soon he finds that the climactic upheaval must be attributed to a much larger force, EGO, THE LIVING PLANET!
