ImpossibleMan

Series: 2 issues 1990
Publisher: Marvel
Cover by Al Milgrom

"Improlog," script by Michael Gallagher, art by Barry Crain.

The Impossible Man decides to take a trip to Earth.

"How Green Was My Villain?," script by Michael Gallagher, art by Barry Crain; The Impossible Man hassles Spider-Man.

"Girls Don't Wanna Have Fun!," script by Mark Gruenwald and Howard Mackie, pencils by Rurik Tyler, inks by Romeo Tanghal. The Impossible Woman attempts to bring a sense of mirth to her daughter with the help of Quasar.

"Impossible but Strange," script by Roy Thomas and Dann Thomas, pencils by Greg Capullo, inks by Chris Ivy; The Impossible Man hassles Doctor Strange.

"Fashion Victims," script by Gregory Wright, art by Jackson Guice. The Impossible Man interrupts a fashion show of the Wasp's designs where She-Hulk and the Invisible Woman are models.

"A Night to Remember!," script by Jim Valentino, pencils by Jim Valentino, inks by Danny Bulanadi; The Impossible Man hassles Punisher.
