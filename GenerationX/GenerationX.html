GenerationX

Series: 76 issues 1994 to 2001, 1 issue 1998
Publisher: Marvel

Chromium cover. Scott Lobdell script. Chris Bachalo art. Mark Buckingham inks. This issue kicks off a brand new generation of X-Men: Husk, Jubilee, M, Chamber, Skin, Penance, Synch, and Mondo led by Banshee and White Queen.

A mysterious mutant called Penance debuts, but is she friend or foe? After arriving to attend the school, Penance lashes out at Generation X. The new classmate may well be their greatest enemy!
