John Byrne's 2112

Series: Graphic Novel 1991
Publisher: Dark Horse
Written by John Byrne
Illustrations by John Byrne

2112 introduces readers to the Next Men, a group of strange people battling for the future of the world -- and what a world it is! Mutated "halflings" are in open revolt, led by the sinister Sathanas, and it's up to the agents of Safeguard, Inc. to sort out the mess! Don't miss the future beyond the Next Men in 2112!
