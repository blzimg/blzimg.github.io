Created by Jim Somerville, 11 issues from 1987 - 1988 published by Aircel

Maelstrom of the Headsmen's Guild arrives to execute wizard Han-Kar-Harsh at the command of Bad-Azz-Brash, sorcerer queen of the metaloids. The wizard casts a spell of power on Maelstrom's axe in return for a final spell (which is done wrong). The injured Maelstrom is found and tended by Kara and her brothers. As the Metaloids destroy everything in their path, their god, the Dark One, appears to Major Kaos and turns him against Bad-Azz. Maelstrom and friends arrive at castle Grim-Blak, home of Bad-Azz. Lith is killed. Major Kaos defeats Bad-Azz. Adma and Opic are killed. The Dark One takes a personal hand in dealing with Bad-Azz and Han-Kar. Kara is killed.
