Kid Komics

Series: 10 issues 1943-1946
Publisher: Timely, Atlas, Marvel
Written by Otto Binder, Stan Lee
Illustrations by Syd Shores, Basil Wolverton, Frank Giacoia

Kid Komics was a World War II magazine published by Marvel predesasor Timely, starring such Marvel heroes as the Human Torch, Bucky Barnes and the Young Allies and Captain Wonder. The series ran from 1943 through to 1946 for 10 issues. This series then changed theme dramatically and continued into Kid Movie Komics with issue 11.

Issue #1 Mister Death enters the world after escaping the mysterious caverns that link our worlds. Local science teacher Professor Jordan, creates a wonder fluid. When he accidentally drops the fluid, spilling it on himself and choking on the fumes, he becomes Captain Wonder. In order to secure his secret identity, he can take a black fluid to revert back to his former self, then developes a ring with a tiny needle and filled wonder fluid allowing him to press the ring and become Captain Wonder when needed. No one but his favorite student Tim Mulrooney knows the truth, wants in on the action and becomes Captain Wonder's side kick, with his own costume. Their first challenge is to defeat Mister Death!
