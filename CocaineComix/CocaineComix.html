Last Gasp 1975. Any comic book titled "Cocaine Comix" has got to be snow-white pure decadence, right? Well, Cocaine Comix does indeed gives us some funny stories about cocaine, but it doesn't provide a blanket endorsement of a drug-crazed lifestyle. This series was developed in part by George DiCaprio, father to his famous actor/son, Leonardo.
