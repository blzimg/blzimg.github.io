Copra is a comic book series created and published entirely by Michel Fiffe, first released at the 2012 Brooklyn Comics and Graphics Festival.

Copra is about a group of mercenary misfits thrust into a macro-revenge mission. A pack of desperate weirdo fugitives who sometimes end up in other dimensions, other times in Tokyo, but they always get brutal no matter where they land.
