Hero Zero

Series: one shot 1994
Publisher: Dark Horse
Written by Michael Eury and Rose Rummel-Eury
Illustrations by Brian Apthorp and Keith Aiken

A desert joyride turns into a deadly nightmare when David MacRae and his friends encounter the life force-absorbing menace of Siphon! Can David's alter-ego Hero Zero save the day -- or will the 100-foot hero end up as the main course for a monstrous mutant?
