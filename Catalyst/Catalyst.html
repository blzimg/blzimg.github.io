Catalyst


Series: on-going, 7 issues
Publisher: 1994 Dark Horse

Written by Eddie Campbell and Pete Ford. Art by Tim Hamilton, Steve Carr and Shane Glines. Cover by Jason Pearson. Fresh from its appearance in Out of the Vortex (1993) #5, super-team Catalyst, rallying behind its omnipotent leader Grace, has declared Golden City's secession from the United States. The government isn't rolling over and playing dead, as they're unleashing a full-scale military assault against the city and its heroes. It's the Agents of Change vs. the U.S. Armed Forces in this action-packed premier issue.
