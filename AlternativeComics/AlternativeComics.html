Founded and owned by Jeff Mason from 1993 until 2007 when publishing was halted. Publishing was resumed in 2012 under new owner Marc Arsenault. Alternative Comics is an American independent graphic novel and comic book publisher currently based in Cupertino, California. In addition to publishing creator-owned titles, Alternative Comics is also a noted publisher of anthologies such as 9-11: Emergency Relief, Hi-Horse, Hickee, Rosetta, and True Porn.
