Adventures of Spiderman


Series: 12 issue cartoon storyline
Illustrator: Alex Saviuk
Writer: Nel Yomtov
Publisher: 1996 Marvel

About: This series presents new stories which mirror, but do not directly adapt, the wall-crawler's cartoon. Not to be confused with the Amazing Spiderman.

Story: Hunted by the Punisher!
Spidey disapproves of the Punisher's violent crime fighting methods and tries to put a stop to them.
