Iceman

Series: 4 issue mini-series 1984
Publisher: Marvel
Written by J.M. DeMatteis
Illustrations by Al Kupperburg and Mike Gustovich
Cover by Mike Zeck and John Beatty

The Fuse!" He's young...He's on his own...He's out for a good time...and he's in more trouble than ever! Bobby Drake, the uncanny Iceman, steps out on his own, no X-Men, No Champions, no Defenders, can Iceman handle his first solo adventure? He better, or the world is doomed.
