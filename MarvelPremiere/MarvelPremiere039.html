Marvel Premiere #39

Series: Marvel Premiere
Characters: Torpedo
Released: Dec 1977
Publisher: Marvel Comics
Writer: Marv Wolfman
Illustrator: Bob Brown, Al Milgrom
Story: Ride a Wild Rocket

The Torpedo inherited his suit from another man, and doesn't know its origins, but he learns this issue that the original's uncle is a Senator Eugene Stivak, and that Stivak has been behind the Rocketeers that have been attacking him. He manages to defeat Kligger, but that just means he's now wanted for attempting to assassinate a US senator.
