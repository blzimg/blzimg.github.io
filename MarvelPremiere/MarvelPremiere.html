Marvel Premiere was a tryout book, intended to determine if a character could attract enough readers to justify launching their own series. It ran for 61 issues from April 1972 to August 1981. Contrary to the concept, the majority of the characters featured had previously appeared in other comics.

Later in the title's run, Marvel Premiere was used to finish stories of characters who had lost their own series including the Man-Wolf in issues #45–46 and the Black Panther in issues #51–53.
