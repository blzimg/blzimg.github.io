Marvel Premiere #20

Series: Marvel Premiere
Characters: Iron Fist
Released: May 1974
Publisher: Marvel Comics
Writer: Roy Thomas
Illustrator: Gil Kane, Arvell Jones
Story: Batroc and Other Assassins

Once again fighting off an attack by members of the Cult of Kara-Kai, Iron Fist decides to once more try and clear his name with Joy Meachum, and so returns to the Meachum building to try to explain to her that her father was murdered by a mysterious Ninja. However, she still will not believe him, and her Uncle Ward has him attacked by his latest paid assassin: Batroc.

Batroc has an army of assassins to try and defeat Iron Fist, however Daniel is able to hold them at bay, and eventually defeat them all when the Ninja suddenly appears and aids him once more. After the fight is over, Daniel demands answers from the Ninja who silently teleports Danny to an alley way before disappearing once more. The Ninja appears in the Wing household where it grabs the book that Lee Wing uncovered, the Ninja suddenly transforms into Lee himself.
