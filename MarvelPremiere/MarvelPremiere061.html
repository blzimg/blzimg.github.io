Marvel Premiere #61 - 1972 Series End

Series: Marvel Premiere
Characters: Star-Lord
Released: August 1981
Publisher: Marvel Comics
Writer: Doug Moench
Illustrator: Tom Sutton
Story: Planet Story

Star-Lord investigates the ruins on what seems to be an abandon planet. He quickly encounters a hostile environment with electric storms, earthquakes and wild winds, which force him to seek shelter underground inside a root-ball.

Believing he is safe from the space storm outside, the root tentacles begin to attack him as the planet itself is a parasite which feeds off of flesh.
