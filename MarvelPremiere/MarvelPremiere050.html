Marvel Premiere #50

Series: Marvel Premiere
Characters: Alice Cooper
Released: Oct 1979
Publisher: Marvel Comics
Writer: Alice Cooper, Jim Salicrup, Roger Stern, Ed Hannigan
Illustrator: Tom Sutton, Terry Austin
Story: From the Inside

As the story opens, Alice is trying to escape from a mental institution when he is caught by Nurse Rozetta, who has orderlies restrain him and throw him in the Quiet Room.

From there, Alice narrates in flashback how he came to be a prisoner in the ward, due to a case of mistaken identity.

Alice finally escapes the asylum, discovers that his loony almost-namesake, Alex Cooper, is running for President, spouting nonsense to swooning crowds. Alice is caught and thrown back into the Quiet Room with his pet snake, Veronica.
