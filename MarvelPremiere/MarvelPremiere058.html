Marvel Premiere #58

Series: Marvel Premiere
Characters: Dr. Who
Released: Feb 1981
Publisher: Marvel Comics
Writer:John Wagner, Pat Mills
Illustrator: Dave Gibbons
Story: Against the Gods

The Doctor finds himself facing the demonic Malevilus on a parallel world where Rome never fell. He defeats them and exiles their leader, Magog, to an empty dimension, leaving the decrepit robot Vesuvias in charge of the Empire.
