Marvel Premiere #37

Series: Marvel Premiere
Characters: 3-D Man
Released: Aug 1977
Publisher: Marvel Comics
Writer: Roy Thomas
Illustrator: Jim Craig
Story: Code-Name: The Cold Warrior

A Skrull, disguised as the vice president, tells Professor Sinkovitz that the 3-D Man is a communist spy and that he should use his machine to become a human weapon to get rid of the 3-D Man. His machine makes him the Cold Warrior,which means that the 3-D Man has yet another challenge to face!
