Marvel Premiere #59

Series: Marvel Premiere
Characters: Dr. Who
Released: April 1981
Publisher: Marvel Comics
Writer: John Wagner, Pat Mills
Illustrator: Dave Gibbons
Story: Full Moon on the Highway

The Doctor arrives in the city of the Zoms, where all emotion is forbidden.

City of the Cursed is reprinted from #9, #10, and #11 of Doctor Who Weekly, and was originally published under the title City of the Damned.

Doctor Who was regularly published as a Marvel UK title. The Doctor and his supporting cast do not exist within the continuity of the mainstream Marvel Universe, though they have crossed over with it from time to time.
