Marvel Premiere #41

Series: Marvel Premiere
Characters: Seeker 3000 Crew
Released: April 1978
Publisher: Marvel Comics
Writer: Doug Moench
Illustrator: Dave Cockrum
Story: The Dying Sun

Learning that their sun is dying, an experimental ship with a handpicked crew of the world's best are going to journey to another universe. However, instead of the corrupt government officials making the trip, Captain Jason Shaw rebels in order to help the people stuck on earth who are destined to die.
