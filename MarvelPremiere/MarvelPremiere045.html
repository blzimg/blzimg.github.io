Marvel Premiere #45

Series: Marvel Premiere
Characters: Man-Wolf
Released: Dec 1978
Publisher: Marvel Comics
Writer: David Kraft
Illustrator: George Perez
Story: Otherwar

Col. John Jameson, NASA astronaut and son of J. Jonah Jameson, human soul is buried within the Man-Wolf. Man-Wolf is the hero of prophecy on a world in another dimension (reached via a portal on the Earth's moon).

John learns that a being named Stargod created the gem that has been making him change into a wolf for so long, and now, in the Other-Realm, he gains Stargod's strength, and becomes his successor.
