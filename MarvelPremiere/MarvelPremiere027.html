Marvel Premiere #27

Series: Marvel Premiere
Characters: Satana
Released: Dec 1975
Publisher: Marvel Comics
Writer: Chris Claremont
Illustrator: Gil Kane
Story: Deathsong (Cover says Deathcry)

Drawn to mystical energies emanating from Chandler, California, Satana saves Deborah Hirsch from being burned at the stake for being a witch. This all serves the purposes of Dansker, a being from another dimension that has possessed Hirsch. The entire episode allows Dansker to take control, and try to take out Satana in the hopes of using her for a spell to allow him to cross over into Earth's dimension.

Satana would summon the power of the Basilisk inside her to slay Dansker once he had acquired a limited physical form on Earth. The powerful blast from this spell causes the car that Dansker is driving to explode and drive off a cliff.
