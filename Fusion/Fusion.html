Fusion

Series: 17 issues 1987-1989, 3 issues 2009
Publisher: Eclipse, Marvel
These two series do not seem to be related.

1987: When crew members of the starship Tsunami get into a bar fight and end up in jail, Captain Indio has to find them and help them escape in "The Soulstar Commission." Script by Steve Barnes and art by Steve Gallacci & Lela Dowling. Issue also has Dr. Watchstop in "Unique Specimen." Script and art by Ken Macklin.

2009: Collision: Part 1 of 3 - Written by Dan Abnett and Andy Lanning. Art by Tyler Kirkham. Covers by Mike Choi, Sonia Oback, and Stjepan Sejic.

The mother of all Top Cow/Marvel crossovers debuts in May! Top Cow's two covert tech teams collide with Marvel's Initiative enforcers, the Mighty Avengers and the Thunderbolts. When a rapid and unregistered Ripclaw starts tearing through a small Northeast town, the Mighty Avengers move in to stop him. When the feral hero escapes, they're drawn into an inevitable conflict with Cyberforce.
