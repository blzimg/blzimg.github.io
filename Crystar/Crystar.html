Cover: Bob Larkin
Series: 11 issues. 1983
Publisher: Marvel

"The Sundered Throne." This giant-sized first issue sets up the premise for the series, which evokes the fantasy and sword-and-sorcery of Conan the Barbarian. Crystar transforms to crystal while Moltar becomes magma, giving both princes special powers. Script by Mary Jo Duffy, pencils by Bret Blevins, inks by Andy Yanchus.
