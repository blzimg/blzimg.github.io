Howard the Human

Series: 2015 Graphic Novel
Publisher: Marvel
Writer: Skottie Young
Illustrator: Jim Mahfood

Spend a day with Howard, a private investigator and the only human living in city full of animals. But not, like, criminals and lowlifes. We're talking elephants, apes, ducks and kitty cats. Catching a case, grinding a few gears, dodging some bullets and almost getting killed by an anthropomorchic monstrosity. It's just another Monday for Howard the Human.
