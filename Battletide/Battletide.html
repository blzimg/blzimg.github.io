Battletide


Series: 4 issue mini series
Illustrator: Geoff Senior, Dell Barras (cover)
Writer: Dan Abnett, Andy Lanning
Publisher: 1992 Marvel

Starring Death's Head II and Killpower. Tag-team wrestling inter-galactic style! It's everyone's favorite cyborg vs. everyone's favorite assassin as Death's Head II and Killpower find themselves in a world where war is an everpresent reality. With Dark Angel, Psylocke, Motormouth, Tuck and Wolverine.
