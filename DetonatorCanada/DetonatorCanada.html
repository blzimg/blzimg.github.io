3 issues started in 1995 published by Detonator Canada, continued at tictoctom.com where they say: Tic Toc Tom started out in 1992 as a Toronto mini-comic. It’s creator, Chetan Patel, originally intended to produce just one issue. Tic Toc Tom issue one was given away free of charge to friends of the artists and anyone who happened to be interested.

As it turned out, more people were interested than he thought. Just a couple of months later, issue two hit the stands and was an immediate hit. From it’s limited beginnings (Tom was the guy who “blew things up”), the character has grown in both scope and depth as artists were given free reign over their personal interpretations of the character.
