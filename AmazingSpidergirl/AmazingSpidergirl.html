Amazing Spider Girl


Series: on-going, 30 issues
Illustrator: Ron Frenz, Sal Buscema
Writer: Tom DeFalco
Publisher: 2006-2009 Marvel

May "Mayday" Parker is the teenage daughter of Peter Parker (Spider-Man) and Mary Jane Watson. First appeared in What If (vol. 2) #105 (February 1998). She later acquired her own ongoing comic book, Spider-Girl, written by DeFalco and drawn by Frenz and Pat Olliffe, which was the longest-running superhero book with a lead female character ever published by Marvel before being relaunched as The Amazing Spider-Girl, and later The Spectacular Spider-Girl. She exists in the MC2 alternative universe.
