Marvel Action Hour

Series: Cartoon, 8 issues IM, 8 issues FF, 1994
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Ron Friedman (Screenplay); Eric Fein (Adaptation)
Illustrations by Anthony Williams

The Marvel Action Hour, later Marvel Action Universe, was a syndicated television block from Genesis Entertainment featuring animated adaptions of Marvel Comics superheroes Fantastic Four, Iron Man and Biker Mice from Mars. The Marvel Action Hour debuted in 1994 and lasted two seasons before being cancelled. The first half of the hour was an episode of Iron Man; the second half, an episode of Fantastic Four. During the first season, Stan Lee introduced the episode.

The short lived comic series was based on episodes of the "Marvel Action Hour" cartoon series.
Iron Man and Force Works try to stop the forces of the Mandarin who have created zombie soldiers to interfere with a new underwater tunnel that Justin Hammer is opening in England.
