Charles Vess (1951) is an American fantasy artist and comics artist who has specialized in the illustration of myths and fairy tales. Famous works include:

  • Starstruck
  • Books of Magic
  • Stardust

    His influences include British "Golden Age" book illustrator Arthur Rackham, Czech Art Nouveau painter Alphonse Mucha, and comic-strip artist Hal Foster, among others. Vess has won several awards for his illustrations.
