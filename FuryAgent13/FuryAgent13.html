Fury Agent 13

Series: 2 issues 1998
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Terry Kavanagh
Illustrations by Ramon Bernado and Ian Akin
Cover by Mike Zeck

The world believes that Nick Fury, Marvel's premiere super-spy, was killed by the Punisher. Tell that to Sharon Carter, the sexy super-spy code-named Agent 13. Carter (Captain America's femme fatale) is determined to find him...so she can kill him!

Sharon Carter's search for the presumed dead Nick Fury drops her smack dab in the middle of World War II--right among the Howling Commandos!
