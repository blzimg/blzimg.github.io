4 issues 1993 Marvel

Tour Of Duty, script by Dan Abnett, pencils by Carlos Pacheco, inks by Oscar Jiminez; Dark Guard is formed by The Time Guardian to defeat Mys-Tech before they subjugate all mankind.

Superconductor explains to Dark Guard the reason for forming the team; War is imminent on the planet Eopia due to the machinations of Mys-Tech unless the newly formed Dark Guard can stop it; But first, Death's Head and Motormorth must rescue Liger and Killpower form the Technarchy and Collapsar. Dark Angel and the UK heroes fight it out with the Leader's bodyguards while Death's Head II and Motormouth try to rescue Killpower and Liger. If things weren't bad enough, MyS-TECH rears its ugly head.
