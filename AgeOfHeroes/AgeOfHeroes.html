About: Halloween did the first 2 issues, being John Hudnall's own publishing title, then Image picked it up starting at #3 for 3 issues also 2 issues of Age of Heroes Special #1-2 in 1997. Some of the stories were featured in color for the first time in Britain’s STRIP Magazine in 2012.

Story: Seven misfits and outcasts, some of them handicapped, are drawn together from all walks of life by fate. They are presented with a problem the gods themselves can’t solve. For the gods are no longer in the world. The actions of these seven usher in a new age. The Age of Heroes…
