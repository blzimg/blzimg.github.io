Cover: Jack Kirby, Vince Colletta

Title becomes Thor; previously Journey Into Mystery #125.

"Whom the Gods Would Destroy!" Script by Stan Lee, pencils by Jack Kirby, inks by Vince Colletta. Thor and Hercules fight over Jane Foster; Odin, still angry because Thor revealed his identity to Jane, removes half Thor's powers by giving 'Odin Power' to Seidring. Hercules defeats Thor.

"The Summons!" Script by Stan Lee, pencils by Jack Kirby, inks by Vince Colletta. The Argonauts are called home to Asgard by Odin. Appearances by Balder, Hogun, Fandral, Volstagg, and Odin.
