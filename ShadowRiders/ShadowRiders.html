Series: 4 issues 1993
Publisher: Marvel

The Screaming Man!, script by John Freeman and Brian Williamson, illustrations by Ross Dearsley.

Huge energy surges from Mys-Tech headquarters attract both Boot of the Shadow Riders and Cable of the X-Men to investigate; Naturally, the pair end up fighting each other instead; When Mys-Tech soldiers attack the fighting duo, one of them is killed and Vorin rides in to take him back to headquarters and resurrect him as the newest Shadow Rider, Grunt. Morbid Visions, script by John Freeman and Brian Williamson, art by Ross Dearsley; Vorin resurrects Warhead member, Boot, to be a Shadow Rider.
