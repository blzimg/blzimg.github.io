The Further Adventures of Indiana Jones #1: Indiana Jones goes after the Ikons of Ikammanen, "statues" of solid gold, after Charlie Dunne is murdered in the attempt. Indiana encounters Edith, Charlie's sister, and Solomon Black, who are both after the Ikons. Edith and Indiana are captured by a cult worshipping the Ikons.
