Series: 34 issues 1983
Publisher: Marvel
Cover art by Terry Austin
Script by John Byrne
Illustrations by John Byrne and Terry Austin

The Further Adventures of Indiana Jones
Indiana Jones goes after the Ikons of Ikammanen, "statues" of solid gold, after Charlie Dunne is murdered in the attempt. Indiana encounters Edith, Charlie's sister, and Solomon Black, who are both after the Ikons. Edith and Indiana are captured by a cult worshipping the Ikons.
