Incomplete Death's Head

Series: 12 issues 1993
Publisher: Marvel
Illustrations by Liam Sharp, Cam Smith

Reprints of some of the original Death's Head's appearances, with new linking tale "Connections" starring Death's Head II, script by John Freeman (plot) and Dan Abnett (script), art by Simon Coleby. Death's Head II and Tuck discover an archive that is dedicated to the study of the original Death's Head.

"The Crossroads of Time" featuring Doctor Who, script by Simon Furman, art by Geoff Senior; During the final engagements in the Robot War, Death's Head was caught in the gravitational well of a collapsing planet, which accelerated him at trans-temporal speeds into the time-space continuum, where he collides with The Doctor's TARDIS.

"Watch Out Dragon's Claws, Here's Death's Head!", script by Simon Furman, art by Geoff Senior.

"Data Retrieval: Dragon's Claws" character profile.

"Introducing... Death's Head" article, art by Bryan Hitch; Profile of Death's Head and listing of Death's Head's United Kingdom and United States appearances.
