Loki

Series: 4 issue mini-series 2004
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Robert Rodi
Illustrations by Esad Ribic

Loki has won. Thor is in chains and the trickster god rules Asgard. Asgardians kneel to their new ruler as his allies jockey for favor in the new regime. Hela desires the soul of Thor. But what will Loki do with his arch foe who is also his step-brother?!
