Marvel Chillers

Series: 7 issues 1975
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Marv Woldman, Bill Mantlo
Cover by Gil Kane
Illustrations by Ed Hannigan, Yong Montano

Like Marvel Premiere, Marvel Chillers features new characters to see if there is enough interest to start a new series, what is sometimes called an umbrella title. For some reason this set did not last long, probably poor sales. The first two issues feature Mordred, the last 5 feature Tigra, who later became a member of the Avengers and a leading member of the west coast Avengers.

Issue #1: Janet Lyton and Grant Whittacker dig a tunnel to try and find the lost tower of Darkhold in England. They work through a storm, which washes out the supports of their tunnel, causing it to cave. In the adjoining chamber they find what they believe is a perfectly preserved human body in a tomb dating back to the Dark Ages. The man comes to life, revealing himself to be Mordred, a magician's apprentice during the final years of Camelot.

Reprint back-up story "It Happened in the Attic!" (from Mystical Tales 7), art by Frank Bolle; A man goes back in time and kills his ancestor.
