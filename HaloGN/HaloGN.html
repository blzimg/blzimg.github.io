Halo Graphic Novel

Series: 2006 Graphic Novel
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Lee Hammock, Jay Faerber, Tsutomu Nihei and Brett Lewis
Illustrated by Simon Bisley, Andrew Robinson, Ed Lee, Tsutomu Nihei, Jean 'Moebius' Giraud
Cover by Phil Hale

Bungie Studios, part of Microsoft Game Studios, has joined forces with Marvel Comics to release a highly-anticipated graphic novel inspired by one of the most popular franchises in the history of the video game industry. The Halo Graphic Novel (HGN) brings the Halo universe to life for the first time in the sequential art medium. It features an anthology of original stories, including one lengthy story, and three shorter tales.
