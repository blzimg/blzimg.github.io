10 issues 2013 Marvel
Illustrator: Bryan Hitch, Writer: Brian Michael Bendis

About: A comic book fictional crossover storyline that involved the conquest of the Earth by the sentient robot tyrant Ultron. The storyline consisted of a 10-issue miniseries plus a number of tie-in books.

This story takes place on two different Earths: the alternate Earth where Ultron annihilated humanity is referred to as Earth-61112, and the alternate reality where Morgan le Fay took over half the world is referred to as Earth-26111.

Story: Ultron has returned and has taken over. Luke Cage and She-Hulk deliberately get captured in a failed attempt to destroy Ultron's base.

Nick Fury's group uses Doctor Doom's Time Platform to go into the future to defeat Ultron before he attacks the present. Wolverine's group goes back in time to kill Henry Pym before he creates Ultron. They both fail.

Logan and Sue return to the present only to find the Kree-Skrull War came to Earth. Months before Ultron's attack, Hank is working in his lab when he is given a package from a hidden Sue. It is recording of himself from the past which explains a complicated virus algorithm to destroy Ultron.

The code is uploaded and the virus destroys him.
