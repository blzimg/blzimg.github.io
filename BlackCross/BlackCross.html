Black Cross Special


Series: one-shot
Illustrator: Chris Warner
Writer: Chris Warner
Publisher: 1988 Dark Horse

The return of one of Dark Horse's most requested characters! Join Conrad, the man branded by his own action as Black Cross, as he makes his way in a violent future. Reprinting the two stories from Dark Horse Presents #1 & #3, with ten pages of new material, and a new cover painted by Chris Warner.
