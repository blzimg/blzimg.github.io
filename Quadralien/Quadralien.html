1988 by Astral and Logotron. Soundtrack by David Whittaker is availabile at YouTube.

Quadralien is a sci-fi puzzle game with shooting, released in 1988 on multiple platforms of the time such as Atari ST and MS-DOS, with a major sound and graphics improvement on the Amiga. The reactor, known as Astra, has 3 levels of six chambers and the reactor core.

Quadraliens have invaded a space station which has a large nuclear reactor. You've been dispatched to remotely control a team of droids to get them to destroy the alien mother which is hiding in the reactor core. You need to decontaminate each level of the space station and avoid meltdown by preventing the core temperature form getting too high or the energy output from going too low, as you proceed.

The droids all differ slightly, for example in their firepower, movement, carrying capacity, and energy supply.
