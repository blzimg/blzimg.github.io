Girls' Life

Series: 6 issues 1954
Publisher: Atlas (Marvel)

Girls' Life is Patsy Walkers own magazine for girls. Patsy Walker first appeared in Miss America Magazine #2 (Nov. 1944), published by Marvel precursor Timely Comics, and became Hellcat in The Avengers #144 (Feb. 1976).

Rachael Taylor plays a version of the character, Trish Walker, in the Marvel Cinematic Universe television series Jessica Jones, which premiered on Netflix in November 2015.
