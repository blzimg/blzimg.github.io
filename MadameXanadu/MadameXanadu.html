Madame Xanadu premiered in Doorway To Nightmare number 1.

1981 issue features the stories "Tarot Readings", "Dance for Two Demons" and "Falling Down to Heaven." with cover by Michael Kaluta

2009 Series: Nimue fails to persuade her sister Morgana from waging war on Camelot. with covers and illustration by Amy Reeder

  • Volume 1 - Disenchanted collects Madame Xanadu (DC, 2008 series) #1-10
  • Volume 2 - Exodus Noir collects Madame Xanadu (DC, 2008 series) #11-15
  • Volume 3 - Broken House of Cards collects Madame Xanadu (DC, 2008 series) #16-23 and part of House of Mystery Halloween Annual (DC, 2009 series) #1
  • Volume 4 - Extra-Sensory collects Madame Xanadu (DC, 2008 series) #24-29.
