1949 #1: Art by Ken Battlefield and others. The path to romantic love presents many obstacles to women. A shocked woman realizes she's coming between the man she loves and the woman he's supposed to marry; Seeking wealth, a woman risks a reputation as a gold-digger; A whirlwind romance leaves a woman wondering what might have been. Plus love advice to readers. I Became the Other Woman; Men Gave Me Jewels; Sincerely Yours.....; My Kiss and Run Romance.
