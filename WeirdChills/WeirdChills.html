Issue #1 Cover art by Bernard Baily.

  • "Insane"; A woman and her lover try to drive her husband crazy by amplifying sounds and claiming he's hearing things, but he discovers their plan.
  • "Last Supper!", script by Bruce Hamilton, art by Sal Trapani; A man feeds an exploding powder to his girlfriend's husband to get rid of him, but it destroys the entire universe.
  • "Hallucinations!"; A man captures his own invisible alter-ego, then finds his alter-ego capturing its own alter-ego.

  • "The Gorilla"; A woman is compelled to marry a gorilla.
