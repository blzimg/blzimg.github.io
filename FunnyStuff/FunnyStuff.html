1944 series by DC, created by Sheldon Mayer the series name changes to "Dodo and the Frog" beginning with issue #80. Sheldon was editor as well as doing pencils and inks for many of the covers and interior stories.

Young D'Artagmouse joins up with the Mouseketeers when King Louis sends them to put down a revolt by the Duke de Maltese.
