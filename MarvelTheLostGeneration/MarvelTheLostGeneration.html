Series: 12 issues 2000
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Roger Stern
Illustrations by John Byrne

Issues published in reverse numerical order.

Introducing the mighty Marvel characters you've never seen before in an epic twelve-part saga! Think that the fight between good and evil began with the debut of the Fantastic Four? Think there weren't costumed crime-fighters before Spider-Man? Think again! Get ready to meet a whole host of previously unknown heroes and villains!

Who are these men and women of mystery? Who are Fireball, Flatiron, Cassandra, Oxbow, Liberty, Captain Hip, Sunshine, the Yankee Clipper, Effigy, Rumor and The Eternal Brain? Why have their exploits remained secret? And what about some of the faces you may recognize, such as Nick Fury, the Watcher, the Sub-Mariner and Doctor Strange? What is their part in this enigmatic adventure? And how will these strange characters reveal new details in our sprawling tapestry of time? The Marvel Universe has a secret... and it's time you learned it!

Dr. Cassandra Locke's fate is revealed in the mind-boggling beginning and end of Marvel's forgotten heroes! An eerie premonition sends the Yankee Clipper and his First Line into the heart of Texas. Can the First Line stop the Cult of Rumor from corrupting the youth of America? Why do Thor, the headstrong god of thunder, and Venus, the groovy goddess of love, make the scene? And what will big daddy Odin have to say about all of this?
