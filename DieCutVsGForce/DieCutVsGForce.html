Die Cut Vs G Force


Series: 2 issues mini-series 1993
Publisher: Marvel

Marvel UK's two new stars team up in an intergalactic limited series, featuring a foil-enhanced cover! Professor Daniel Jones dons a gravity suit to explore MyS-TECH wormholes, but when Die-Cut shows up to help him out, Jones is lost in another dimension. Written by John Freeman. Art by John Royle and Tim Perkins. Cover by Liam Sharp.
