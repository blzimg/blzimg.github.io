Dark Horse Insider ran for 48 issues beginning in January 1991. Issue #2 features a 2-page Aliens vs. Predator 2 strip by Randy Stradley and Chris Warner; 4-page preview of Cyberantics by Stanislaw Mayakovsky and Rick Geary; 6-page preview of The Terminator: Hunters and Killers by Toren Smith, Bill Jaaska and Dan Panosian; 4-page preview of The Secret of the Salamander by Jacques Tardi; Front cover by Dan Barry, back cover by Kelley Jones.

Issue #4 features a 2-page Aliens vs. Predator 2 strip by Randy Stradley and Chris Warner; 4-page preview of Hard Looks #2 by Andrew Vachss, Randy Stradley and David Lloyd; 6-page preview of Aliens: Newt's Tale #1 by Mike Richardson, Jim Somerville and Brian Garvey; 4-page preview of Marshal Law: Super Babylon by Pat Mills and Kevin O'Neill; Front cover by Eddie Campbell (from The Eyeball Kid #1), back cover by Kevin O'Neill (from Marshal Law: Super Babylon).
