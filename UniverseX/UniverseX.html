Series: 14 issues 2000 - 2001
Painted cover by Alex Ross
Publisher: Marvel

The EARTH X saga continues with this double-sized opening chapter of UNIVERSE X! Reed Richards thought that the Earth was saved. Galactus was gone. The Celestials were defeated. But when three old friends (the Angel, Nighthawk and Gargoyle) meet, they are accosted by a nightmarish vision of the future of the Marvel Universe.

They learn that the rebirth of Captain Mar-Vell won't bring peace, but a terrible civil war for the future of mutanthood.
