Hero Warrior of the Mystic Realms

Series: 6 issues 1990
Publisher: Marvel
Cover art by Steve Purcell
Script by David Michelinie
Illustrations by Steve Purcell, Whilce Portacio, Sam de la Rosa

A Hero Born! Meet the most unlikely hero of all in this saga of a man's unending obligation to combat evil, an obligation that begins after he is killed! Cody discovers in the spiritual realm what is necessary to become a hero; But can he endure the sacrifice that must be made? Cody goes to the future and finds himself in a soul-destroying combat.
