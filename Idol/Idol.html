Idol

Series: 3 issues 1992
Publisher: Epic (Marvel)

Cover by Ron Randall. Untitled story, script by Gerard Jones and Steve Mattsson, pencils by Ron Randall, inks by Rick Magyar; Contractually obligated to film a sequel to a B-movie which was her first acting job, popular actress Rachel Ahmed hires a film making crew to finish the movie so she can return to work on her other projects; However, the filming hits minor snags with some creative differences; After these are resolved, more problems arise in the form of accidents occurring on the undersea Military installation which previously had been owned and operated by the Soviets for undisclosed research.
