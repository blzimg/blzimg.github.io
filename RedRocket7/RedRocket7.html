Series: 7 issues 1997
Publisher: Dark Horse
Created by Mike Allred

Red Rocket 7 is a seven-issue comic book limited series by Mike Allred, published in 1997 by Dark Horse Comics. It has been collected into trade paperback (ISBN 1569713472), most recently with a tenth anniversary hardcover edition from Image Comics. Nominated for the "Best Limited Series" Eisner Award. Mike Allred also released an album and a film, Astroesque, tied to the comic. They are meant to form a loose trilogy.
