13 issue Horror anthology series by Toby Comics in 1952, also a 3 episode TV series by Harvest Moon in 2015.

Jessica Felice plays Lilith Death, the hostess of these tales of blood treachery, a fun-loving and sexy spirit that melts the screen with her sensual and yet deadly introductions. Jessica also plays various characters through Episode #3 of this series.
