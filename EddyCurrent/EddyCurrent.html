Eddy Current is a twelve issues comic-book series created in 1987 by Ted McKeever and published by Mad Dog Graphics. This and Transit were later tied into McKeever's Metropol world.

  • Eddy Current (12 issue limited series, Mad Dog Graphics 1987-1988, trade paperback, hardcover, Dark Horse Comics reprint in 1991.
  • Eddy Current (3 part re-issue, Atomeka Press, 2005)
