3 issues 1997 Marvel
Illustrator: Cedric Nocon, Roger Cruz, Wellington Diaz
Writer: Keith Giffen

Bad Karma: Viper decides to have her revenge against Karma by contracting Spiral to abduct her siblings and work on them in the Body Shop; Beast, Karma and Cannonball begin their search for the twins only to be assisted by Spiral.

Body Shopping: Beast and his companions find Karma's siblings, only to learn that Spiral has turned the children into adults with deadly powers; Under Viper's control, the Coy twins fight their sister and her friends.

Closing Shop: Beast and his pals continue to try and wrest control of Karma's brother and sister away from Viper, which is hard when the twins insist on trying to punch you in the face while you are attempting to rescue them; The ensuing battle turns Viper against her ally Spiral and brings down the Body Shop; Leong and Nga are stuck in their adult bodies and leave to find their future with Karma.
