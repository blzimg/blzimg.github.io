Created by John Peck, illustrator of Comix: A History Of Comic Books In America. Listed in the Jay Kennedy Underground Price Guide #1205-1209

John "The Mad" Peck was born in 1942 in New York City. He is an American underground cartoonist, rock poster artist, and disc jockey. His most famous poster is a 1978 comic book-style poster that starts with the line, "Providence, Rhode Island, where it rains two days out of three except during the rainy season when it snows like a bitch." The poster has been reprinted many times and is widely available for purchase throughout Rhode Island.
