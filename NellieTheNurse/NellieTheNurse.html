Series: 36 issues 1945-1952
Publisher: Atlas, Marvel

Humor and romance anthology featuring Nellie the Nurse. Nellie and the other characters are realistically portrayed. After a couple of years the comic moved further in the 'good girl' direction, with slightly more stylized, cartoon-like characters, and humor more typical of teen books like Archie.
