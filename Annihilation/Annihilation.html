6 issues 2006, 3 issues 2007 Marvel
Illustrator: Andrea Divito, Writer: Keith Giffen

Nova and Starlord lead a multi-world army against the forces of Annihilus as they attack and destroy world after world. After surviving a particular attack and capturing one of Annihilus' generals, Nova learns that Galactus has been killed; Annihilus is working with Thanos.
