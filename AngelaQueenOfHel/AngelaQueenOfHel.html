Angela Queen Of Hel


Series: on-going, 7 issues
Illustrator: Julian Totino Tedesco
Writer: Marguerite Bennett
Publisher: 2015 Marvel

HEL HATH A NEW FURY. Stolen from ASGARD, exiled from HEVEN, and robbed of SERA, her greatest companion, ANGELA plunges into the depths of blackest HEL to save her friend. Once there, the lost princess of Asgard will carve out a realm of her very own. All hail ANGELA, THE NEW QUEEN OF HEL!
