Devil's Due Publishing (devilsdue.net) was formed in 1999 as both a commercial art studio and a small press comic book publisher. Devil's Due Publishing formed a partnership with Image Comics in 2001 with the successful revamp of the 1980's G.I. Joe franchise. The company outgrew its publisher by the end of 2003, leaving to map out its own destiny. In 2015, Devil's Due merged with the publisher 1First Comics to become Devil's Due / 1First Comics, LLC.
