Mikeross Publications 1954 series, New Comics Group 1987 series. Satire anthology series that looks like early EC Mad Magazine.

Get Lost was quite unique when it was released. During the time of all of the Mad comic book imitations, all were produced by the major comic book companies of the time. For instance, Harvey had Flip, Atlas had Crazy, Charlton had Eh!

Get Lost was produced by Ross Andru and Mike Esposito for their own Mikeross Publications. The humor and style was so similar to Mad that E.C. made mention of it on the cover of E.C.’s Panic #4, where it was referred to as “Get Stung”!
