I heard about her from the Katie Skelly Interview on Cartoonist Kayfabe. All I know about this artist is from her web site Dreamyguts her Instagram Page and from the Katie Skelly interview in the Comics Journal The Comics Journal Interview.

Face Fatigue is a self-published comic by the artist. This 8.5 x 5.5 sized, 10 page, body and psychological horror comic comments on many things. It starts on the ritualistic nature of checking your face when you have a flawed relationship with yourself, tentatively comments on Internet addiction, looking for saviors in media and beauty standards. All very quickly and, hopefully, creepily. Accompanying it is a poppy palette of pink and blue, with accents of red.
