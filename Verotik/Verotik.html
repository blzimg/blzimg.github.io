Verotik is an American mature-themed comic book company founded in August 1994 by heavy metal/horror punk musician Glenn Danzig (vocalist and songwriter for bands The Misfits, Samhain and Danzig). The start-up was originally led by editor-in-chief Valarie Jones (Eclipse, First Comics, Majestic), who left at the end of 1995. She was followed by Steven Wardlaw. The comics are aimed toward adult readers as they often contain imagery of a sexual and/or violent nature. 'Verotik' is a portmanteau created by Glenn Danzig from the words 'violent' and 'erotic'.
