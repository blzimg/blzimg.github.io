Series: 11 issues 1953
Publisher: Atlas, Marvel

Science Fiction, Horror Anthology.

Issue #1:

  • "One Head Too Many!" script by Stan Lee, art by Bill Everett.
  • The Man Who Couldn't Move, script by Stan Lee, art by George Tuska. A woman hires a man to kill her paralyzed husband making it look like a car accident, but he gets his sleeve caught in the door and goes over the cliff with the car.
  • Poor Mr. Watkins, script by Stan Lee, art by Werner Roth. A practical joker torments a timid librarian until he transforms into a monster.
  • They Wait in Their...Dungeon!, art by Russ Heath. After an execution in the gas chamber, a sadistic warden faces a revolt by the inmates and attempts to flee downstairs.
