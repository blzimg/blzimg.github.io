6 issues, 1988 - 1995 by Fantagraphics.

The misadventures of Dennis Wordens bitter, stick-figure alter ego. FTW. Stickboy loses his job for stealing a copy machine and must face the torment of a new job search. In a dream, he gets to tell Uncle Sam whats what. Story, art and covers by Dennis Worden.
