I, Paparazzi

Series: Graphic Novel 2002
Publisher: Vertigo
Written by Pat McGreal
Illustrations by Stephen John Phillips, Steven Parke

Utilizing photographs instead of illustrations, I, PAPARAZZI offers an insightful look into the world of celebrity-chasing photographers while telling a story of intrigue and conspiracy. When paparazzo Jake "Monster" McGowran and his camera have a run-in with a temperamental celebrity, his entire world is changed. Events unfold that have the relentless photographer caught in the middle of a conspiracy that he must unravel before it costs him his life. With interesting characters and well-crafted dialogue, this book is a whirlwind ride through life in the public eye and the sacrifices and compromises that must be made to rise to the top.
