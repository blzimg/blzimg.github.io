Series: Graphic Novel
Publisher: Vertigo (DC)
Written by Harvey Pekar
Illustrations by Dean Haspiel

For the first time, Harvey Pekar tells the story of his childhood as the son of Jewish immigrants. This graphic novel is the universal tale of a young man's search for himself through the frustrations, redemptions and complexities of ordinary life.

Harvey Pekar (1939-2010) was a comic book writer and author of the autobiographical American Splendor series, which was adapted into an Academy Award nominated film. Pekar was also a prolific jazz and book critic.
