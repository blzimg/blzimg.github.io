Series: 18 issues 2005, 3 TPB collections
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Kurt Busiek, Fabian Nicieza
Illustrations by Tom Gummet

Dual numbered, continuing the number from previous Thunderbolts series.

From the ashes of the Avengers, a new group of heroes springs forth, all of whom happen to be former villains! NEW THUNDERBOLTS signals a return to greatness for one of Marvel's longest-running titles of the last decade! But does this new team have the backbone to support the hopes and dreams of an entire city? Guest-starring Spider-Man and Wolverine!
