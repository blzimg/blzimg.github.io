Day of the Defenders


Cover: Erik Larsen
Writer: Roy Thomas, Ross Andru
Illustrators: Marie Severin, Herb Trimpe
Series: one-shot reprint magazine
Publisher: Marvel

In conjunction with DEFENDERS #1, Marvel reprints the hard-to-find stories that led to the formation of the "non-team"!

Dr. Strange and Namor battle the Mindless Ones (from SUB-MARINER #22).
The Master of the Mystic Arts and the Hulk team to confront the Night-Crawler (from INCREDIBLE HULK #126).
All three Defenders unite for the first time (from MARVEL FEATURE #1).
