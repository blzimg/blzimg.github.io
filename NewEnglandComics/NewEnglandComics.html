New England Comics was founded in 1988. They are a comic book retail chain and publisher headquartered in Brockton, Massachusetts, USA. They are known for publishing The Tick comic books. They have locations in Quincy, Brookline, Brockton, Cambridge, Malden, New Bedford and Norwood. The chain is known for owning the superhero The Tick. In 2016, The Tick became the star of a TV show streamed through Amazon Video services.
