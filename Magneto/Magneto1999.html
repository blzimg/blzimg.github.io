The Magneto War

Series: one-shot 1999
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Alan Davis, Fabian Nicieza
Illustrations by Lee Weeks, Dan Green

The X-Men take the fight to Magneto. Joseph sacrifices himself to save the world from being destroyed. The U.N. gives Maggie sole rulership of Genosha in return for his agreement never to launch another attack against humanity. Cont. from Uncanny #367; Cont. in Magneto Rex #1. 38 pgs.
