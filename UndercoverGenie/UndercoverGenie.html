Series: Graphic Novel 2003
Written and Illustrated by Kyle Baker
Publisher: Vertigo

A one-of-a-kind collection of Kyle Baker's musings and ruminations on the pitfalls of relationships, the absurdity of cultural assimilation, and much, much more.

This collection is a combination of a short story anthology of random Kyle Baker shorts and a Kyle Baker Sketch book. Kyle Baker (born 1965) is an American cartoonist, comic book writer-artist, and animator known for his graphic novels and for a 2000s revival of the series Plastic Man. Baker has won numerous Eisner Awards and Harvey Awards for his work in the comics field.
