Publishers of the finest Independent specialty comics you can find! Created and run by Tim Tyler, a 30 year vet of the Independent Comics Scene. After leaving the Joe Kubert School in 1986, Tyler immediately began working in the comics field, working on and or creating such classic titles as Blood Reign, Faust, Razor Dreadstar, Hades and more. Currently publishing the Horror rag, FIENDS and working on the Blood Reign re-launch series as well as other publications.
