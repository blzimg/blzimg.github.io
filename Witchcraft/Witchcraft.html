6 issues in 1952 by Avon

Heritage of Horror!", script by Sol Cohen, art by Joe Kubert. Weird events occur when Julian Daniels puts brush to canvas.
"Devil-Music!", pencils by Gene Fawcette, inks by Vince Alascia. A beautiful young singer discovers what brings evil to life.
"This Ship Is Doomed!" The Indies Queen and its passengers were destined for disaster. "Demon's Bargain!" text story.
"Vengeance of the Ouanga!" Bob Grant carried a dead killer's good luck charm-the bones of a hand and arm-which leads to terror.
