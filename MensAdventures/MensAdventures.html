Series: 25 issues (#4-29) 1950-1954
Publisher: Atlas, Marvel
Cover by Syd Shores

Numbering continues from True Adventures #3. Adventure anthology to #8, war anthology #9–20, SF/Horror anthology #21–26, it became a superhero anthology from #27, along with the cancellation of four other such anthologies, reducing Atlas's SF line from thirteen books to eight. It included Human Torch, Captain America and Sub-Mariner features from #27.
