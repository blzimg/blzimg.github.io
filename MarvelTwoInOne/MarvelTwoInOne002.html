Marvel Two-In-One


Script by Steve Gerber
Illustrations by Gil Kane, Joe Sinnott

The Sub-Mariner, Wundarr, and Namorita guest star in "Manhunters from the Stars!"
When the stranger named Wundarr falls from the heavens he is discovered by the beautiful Atlantean Namorita, cousin to the Savage Sub-Mariner. A misunderstanding sets the powerful Wundarr against the ever-lovin' blue-eyed Thing. Then, when the Monarch of the Seven Seas shows up, things just get worse from there.
