2099 A.D. Apocalypse


Series: one-shot
Illustrator: Mark Buckingham
Writer: Warren Ellis
Publisher: 1995 by Marvel

About: The world of 2099 is a cyberpunk dystopia, similar to the world of Blade Runner. North America is a corporate police state ruled by a few huge megacorporations, most notably Alchemax, which owns the private police force the Public Eye. Doom 2099, revealed to be, in fact, Victor Von Doom, would take over the United States.
