King Tiger and Motorhead

Series: 2 issues 1996
Publisher: Dark Horse
Written by D.G. Chichester
Illustrations by Karl Waller and Eric Shanower

They came to Grinnin' Jimbo's looking for amusement. They found murder. Nothing can bring them back, but King Tiger can bring them vengeance. And he won't have to do it alone. From the pages of Ghost, King Tiger joins Motorhead for a devil hunt down New Orleans way! It'll be anything but Big Easy!
