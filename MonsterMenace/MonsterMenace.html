Series: 4 issue mini-series reprints 1993
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Stan Lee
Illustrations by Steve Ditko
Cover by Kyle Baker

Reprints from Tales to Astonish.

Issue #1 reprints Tales to Astonish #7. A sculptor builds two statues, one representing good and the other, evil, but the evil statue comes to life and attacks him. The good statue saves him, and both of them fall into the swamp.
