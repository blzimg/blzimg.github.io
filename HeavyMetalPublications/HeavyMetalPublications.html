Heavy Metal Publications was created to produce Heavy Metal Magazine in 1977, but they quickly gained enough momentum to release the other books listed below. Alien: The Illustrated Story is a sixty-four page critically acclaimed graphic novel adaptation of the 1979 science-fiction horror film Alien published by Heavy Metal in 1979. It is the first comic from the Alien franchise, a major critical and commercial success and was the first comic to ever be listed on the New York Times Bestsellers list.
