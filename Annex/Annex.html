Annex


Series: on-going, 4 issues
Illustrator: Walter McDaniel
Writer: Jack C. Harris
Publisher: 1994 Marvel

#1 Enemy Unknown. Someone is trying to get the Annex armor, and they don't care if Alex Ellis has to die for them to get it; Spider-Man guest-stars.

#2 Circuits of Vengeance. Brace has taken the Annex armor from Alex. Dr. Barto has a new device, that when inserted in Alex's body, will allow him to recreate the armor by just imagining it. Spotlight on The Secret Defenders.

#3 Ascent. Annex searches for Dr. Barto, and battles Platoon.

#4 Passing of a Hero. Annex and Spidey take A.I.M. at Brace. Stan's Soapbox, talking about why he uses "Excelsior." Spotlight on Fantastic Force.
