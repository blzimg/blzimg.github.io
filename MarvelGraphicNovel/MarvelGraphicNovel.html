Issue #1 THE DEATH OF CAPTAIN MARVEL, story and art by Jim Starlin. Watch the dying hero tragically succumb to the cancer that ravages his body. Captain Marvel succumbs to the cancer that he acquired from battling Nitro in Captain Marvel Vol 1 34. Earth's heroes pay their respects. This is one of the greatest super-hero stories ever told, by one of the medium's master storytellers - Jim Starlin! Featuring appearances by Thanos, Spider-Man, Hulk, Fantastic Four, the Avengers, X-Men, Defenders, Inhumans, Daredevil, and more.
