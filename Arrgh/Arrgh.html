Arrgh!


Series: on-going, 5 issues
Illustrator: Marie Severin, Tom Sutton
Writer: Russ Jones
Publisher: 1974-1975 Marvel

Crazy-funny humor tales that will make you go Arrgh! Includes parody of the classic Universal monsters, it was a horror/mystery mag with a humorous twist, a love-letter to the classic EC Comics in the guise of the love-child of Tales from the Crypt and Mad.
