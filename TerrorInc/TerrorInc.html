Series: 13 issues 1992
Written by D.G. Chichester
Illustrated by Jorge Zaffino
Publisher: Marvel

Introducing Terror, a supernatural assassin who steals the abilities of others by robbing and using their body parts. Featuring the villainy of the demon Bezeel and the birth of a new supernatural super hero. Meanwhile, Terror makes his first dramatic move at invading Bezeel's domain.

Terror must battle an armada from Hell. So he recruits an involuntary ally by releasing Damion Hellstorm's dark soul into his ally's body, turning him into Hellfire. Terror and his new ally, Hellfire, must defeat Beelzeboul's satanic forces to bring down the evil Barbatos and uncover the secrets to Hellfire's mysterious past.
