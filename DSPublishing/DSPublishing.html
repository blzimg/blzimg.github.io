D.S. Publishing Company, Inc. entered the publishing market around 1941, with several 'song sheets' (slim magazines, which reprinted the lyrics of popular songs under license from A.S.C.A.P.), which were popular at the time. They published comics from 1947 until 1950, with a total of 18 series and 77 issues.
