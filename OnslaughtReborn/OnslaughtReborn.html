Series: 5 issues 2007
Publisher: Marvel

When the Scarlet Witch destroyed most of the mutants on M-Day, she unwittingly unleashed Onslaught back into the world. Franklin and the Thing are playing a video game when Onslaught possesses the Torch and uses him to attack the boy who helped defeat him.

Afraid for his family, Franklin uses his green orb to transport himself to the Heroes Reborn universe where he quickly meets up with Bucky. Onslaught follows.
