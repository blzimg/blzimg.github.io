Handbook of the Conan Universe

Series: one shot 1986
Publisher: Marvel

Wraparound Cover by Mike Kaluta. Written by Alan Zelenetz. Artwork by John Buscema, Bob Camp, Ernie Chan, Vince Colletta, Mike Docherty, Armando Gil, Geof Isherwood, Andy Kubert, Gary Kwapisz, Dave Simons, Vincent Waller and Mary Wilshire. Featuring encyclopedia-style entries for the people and places of the world of Conan the Barbarian, from Aesgaard to Zingara. Plus: 1-page introduction and tribute to Robert E. Howard.
