Series: 1948
Publisher: Timely, Marvel
Written and Illustrated (mostly) by Harvey Kurtzman

Title changes to Mitzi's Boyfriend, then Mitzi's Romances.
Issue #1: A famous explorer arrives in Hillville to give a lecture at the high school.
Characters include Mitzi Martin, Daisy Dillwater, Toni, Sir Joshua Digby, Jeffrey Droll, Eb, Principal Droll.
