Hook

Series: Movie Adaptation 1992
Publisher: Marvel

Marvel published this adaptation in three forms: limited series comic book, softcover collection, and magazine. Marvel considered the comic book the first publication, though they apparently came out in this order: magazine, softcover, comic book.

Adapted by Charles Vess. Art by John Ridgway, Denis Rodier, Gray Morrow, Ray Iago, Anna-Maria Cool, and Craig Hamilton. Cover by Charles Vess. This beautifully colored volume adpats the major motion picture starring Robin Williams, Dustin Hoffman, and Julia Roberts. Find out what happened to Peter Pan after he left Neverland, and bare witness to his triumphant return home. Continuing the story that has enchanted readers for generations.
