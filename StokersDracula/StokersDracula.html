Series: 4 issue mini series 2004
Publisher: Marvel

Harker travels to Castle Dracula to conduct some business with his client.

After exploring the castle and discovering there is no way out, Harker is startled when he can't see Dracula's reflection in his shaving mirror.

During the night, three females enter Harker's room but Dracula intervenes just before one of them attempts to bite Harker's neck.

Harker spots Dracula leaving the castle in clothes taken from his own bag and he realizes that whatever wickedness Dracula has planned will be attributed to him.
