The Age of Aquarius: Book One: Paleonecromancy. Story, art and cover by Rick McCollum. Ashley Dust uses her powerful magic to protect the dead from the living, and the living from the dead. Ashley communes with an alien spirit, alienating the rest of the dead, and only the souls of H.P. Lovecraft and Robert E. Howard will have anything to do with her.

The Age of Aquarius: Book Two: Gods in the Machines. Story, art and cover by Rick McCollum. Ashley Dust uses her powerful magic to protect the dead from the living, and the living from the dead. Ashley battles a scorpion-tailed cyborg monstrosity.

The Age of Aquarius: Book Three: Blood on Her Hands. Story, art and cover by Rick McCollum. Ashley Dust uses her powerful magic to protect the dead from the living, and the living from the dead. Ashley explores a future Cincinnati that teeters on the edge of destruction!

The Age of Aquarius: Book Four: History Hurts (It Burns Without End). Story by Rick McCollum and Paula Robinson. Art and cover by Rick McCollum. Ashley Dust uses her powerful magic to protect the dead from the living, and the living from the dead. Ashley faces the weird and horrifying at the edges of our reality. Final issue of the series.
