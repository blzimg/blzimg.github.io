Hammer

Series: 4 issues 1997
Publisher: Dark Horse
Story and art by Kelley Jones

When ancient death cults and mushroom gods vie with fast food restaurants and infomercials for dominance of mankind, a 1500-year-old warrior must return to life to preserve the natural order. Owing equally to 1930s pulp fiction and 1970s horror comics, fan-favorite Batman artist Kelley Jones introduces an outlandish hero in an eerie, action-packed tale on the cutting edge of adventure comics.
