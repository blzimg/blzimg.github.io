Bigg Time


Series: one-shot, Graphic Novel
Illustrator: Ty Templeton
Writer: Ty Templeton
Publisher: 2002 Vertigo (DC)

What do you do when your "guardian angel" just happens to hate you and is determined to ruin your life? That's what happens to Lester Bigg in BIGG TIME, an original graphic novel from VERTIGO. It's a hilarious dark comedy from the twisted mind of cartoonist Ty Templeton!
