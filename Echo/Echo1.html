Echo of Futurepast

Series: 9 issues 1984-1986
Publisher: Continuity Graphics
Cover by Neal Adams

Continuity's attempt to do a Heavy Metal/Epic Illustrated style anthology comic.

Issue #1 Bucky O'Hare (script by Larry Hama, art by Michael Golden), Tippie Toe Jones (script by Lynley Farley, art by Louis Mitchell with Neal Adams), Frankenstein (script and art by Neal Adams), Virus (script and art by Jean Teule), and Mudwogs (script and art by Arthur Suydam).
