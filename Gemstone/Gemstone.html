Since 1994, Gemstone Publishing is a Division of Geppi's Entertainment Publishing & Auctions (Stephen A. Geppi). They are the current publisher of the Overstreet Comic Book Price Guide (Robert M. Overstreet; in print annually since 1970) and various other guides/publications including later issues of the reference magazine, 'Comic Book Marketplace.' Gemstone inherited publisher Russ Cochran's EC/Entertaining Comics' color reprint series in 1994 (which were also compiled into squarebound anthologies). Much like Gladstone, Gemstone is associated with reprinting various EC and Walt Disney comics series. Website Gemstonepub.com
