It's bad enough a raid on a technological super-base goes horribly wrong for the Warheads; The portal that took them to the base also went back in time so if Liger doesn't handle the tragic events just so, time and space are at risk.

Warheads Back Dawn: The premiere issue has a red metallic cover with an embossed logo. When the Warheads try to stop animals with an insatiable thirst for blood, some of them do not survive the battle. Written by Craig Huston. Art by Charlie Adlard and James Hodgkins. Cover by Charlie Adlard.
