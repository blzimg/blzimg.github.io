Painted cover art by Tennyson Smith. Mary Shelly's Frankenstein, script by Gary Friedrich (adaptation) and Mary Shelley (original story), art by Mike Ploog.

Robert Walton IV leads an expedition into the Arctic to retrieve Frankenstein's Monster which has been frozen into the ice. He relates to the cabin boy how the monster was created.

The Origin of the Man-Thing, script by Roy Thomas and Gerry Conway, art by Gray Morrow. Gargoyle Every Night, script by Bernie Wrightson and Roy Thomas, art by Bernie Wrightson.
