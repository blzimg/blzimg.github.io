Series: 4 issues mini-series 1993
Publisher: Marvel
Written and Illustrated by Howard Chaykin

Epic's Heavy Hitters line unleashes Midnight Men, a 4-issue limited series. Issue #1 premieres with a cover embossed with metallic ink. When a rogue/thief from NYC runs up against the Midnight Men, an LA based vigilante order, he is reluctantly caught up in their brutal exploits.

Events in the last issue brought Barnett face to face with the Midnight Men. Now, with one of their members down, the Midnight Men recruit Barnett into their ranks.

Barnett Pasternak takes on the mantle of the Midnight Men. His first assignment is a dark web of intrigue involving stolen microfilm and political shenanigans.

Howard Chaykin's blockbuster limited series concludes. Pasternak hurls a crushing blow at the culprits behind the microfilm heist. Then he makes a decision that will touch his life forever.
