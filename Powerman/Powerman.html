The series starts off as "Luke Cage Hero for Hire" (#1-16), "Luke Cage Powerman" (#17-47), "Powerman" (#48-49), then "Power Man and Iron Fist" (#50-125) without re-numbering. Issue 125 features the death of Iron Fist. 1996 started a new "Heroes For Hire" (19 issues), the again in 2006 featuring Daughters of the Dragon. 2011 Power Man and Iron Fist 5 issue limited series.
