Filthy Rich


Series: 2010 Graphic Novel
Publisher: Vertigo Crime (DC)

Written by Brian Azzarello
Art by Victor Santos
Cover by Lee Bermejo

Richard 'Junk' Junkin has always lived on the wrong side of trouble. A former pro football star whose career was cut short by injury (and a nasty gambling problem), Junk now spends his time dreaming of what might have been, selling cars in Jersey and lusting after the boss's unbelievably spoiled, unbelievably sexy and unbelievably rich daughter, Victoria.

So when the boss asks him to be Victoria's personal bodyguard while she tears up the New York City club scene, Junk leaps at the chance. But before long, he's finds that Victoria wants a lapdog and not a chaperone, someone who's going to do all of her dirty work, all of it, someone who wants to get filthy rich.
