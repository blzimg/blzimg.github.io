The Marvel version has numbering that continues from Jeanie Comics (Marvel, 1947 series) #27.

A comic book for romance stories starring cowgirls. Stories include "Ride Fast for Wyoming," "Calamity Jane," "A Kiss for Captain Lobo," "Halfway House," and "Beneath the Outlaw Moon."

While the stories do generally end in "and we'll be in love foreeeever" way you expect, in half of them in this collection, the love story is barely even on the page. Instead, you get eight to ten pages of women shooting things, riding horses, and (in the big finale) setting fire to an entire ranch as revenge against a crooked ranch owner.

There's still the gender expectations of the time, of course, but these Cowgirls are so fun and independent and tough until they run off with their fella that they're easy to love and root for.
