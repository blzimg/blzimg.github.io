Since 1998 Web Site: JayHosler.com

Jay Hosler is a neurobiologist who studies olfactory processing in honey bees. During his college and graduate school days, he goofed around as the cartoonist for his university newspapers. Since then he has made it is goal to use his cartoons and comic books to explain biology to anyone that will read them.
