Girl Comics / Girl Confessions

Series: 35 issues from 1949 to 1954
Publisher: Timely (Marvel)

Girl Comics changed names to Girl Confessions at issue #13 in 1952.

Issue #1 features: "No Escape." Prissie Welldon runs a restaurant in Tough Luck, Alaska, where she finds love. Also includes: "The Other Woman!"; "Love Is Where the Heart Is!"; "Death Be My Destiny!"; "I Lost My Heart!"; "Love Comes First"; and "Dan Cupid and You." Photo cover
