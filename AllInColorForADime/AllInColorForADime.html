272 pages published by Arlington House in 1970, reprinted by Krause Publications in 1997. written by Richard A. Lupoff and Don Thompson.

Eleven comic-book collectors recall their childhood comics heroes - more than 100 in all. The 1997 reprint sports an all-new introduction by Comics Buyer's Guide Editor Maggie Thompson and includes 16 pages of full-color comic-book art.
