The Gilberton Company, Inc. was an American publisher best known for the comic book series Classics Illustrated. Beginning life as an imprint of the Elliot Publishing Company in 1941. The company became independent in 1942, before being sold to the Frawley Corporation in 1967. The company ceased publishing in 1971.

Titles

  • Classic Comics (1941)
  • Bomber Comics (1944)
  • Classics Illustrated (1947)
  • Classics Illustrated Junior (1953)
  • Classics Illustrated Special Issue (1955)
  • The World Around Us (1958)
