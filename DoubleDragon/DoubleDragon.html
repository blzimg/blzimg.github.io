Double Dragon


Series: Video Game Adaptation 5 issue mini-series
Publisher: Marvel

Video Game Stars in Their Comics Debut! "Enter the Double Dragon." Written by Dwayne McDuffie, pencils by Tom Raney, inks & colors by Brad Vancata, lettering by Pat Brosseau.

The source of the Double Dragons' mystical powers is stolen. To get back the Dragon Statue they must face Stelth and his legions of thugs...all without their powers.
