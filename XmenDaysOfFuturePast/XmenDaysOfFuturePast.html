X-Men Days of Future Past


Writer: Chris Claremont
Illustrators: John Byrne, Terry Austin
Series: Reprint Collection
Publisher: 1989, 2004 Marvel

Collects Uncanny X-Men #138-143 and X-Men Annual #4. It's Claremont and Byrne's final story arc on the Merry Mutants and it's one of their best. This is a New Extended Edition with previously uncollected material.

Nightcrawler and Wolverine must team-up with members of Alpha Flight if they hope to survive an encounter with the deadly Wendigo. Then, in a post-apocalyptic future the mutant-hunting killing machines known as the Sentinels rule America with an iron fist. Almost all mutants, super-heroes, and villains have been exterminated. Only a handful remain to fight agianst their oppressive robotic overseers. Now, Kate Pryde must travel back in time and warn the X-Men of the coming danger and maybe prevent this horrible future from ever taking place.
