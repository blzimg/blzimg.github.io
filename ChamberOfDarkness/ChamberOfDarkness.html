Chamber Of Darkness


Series: on-going, 8 issues
Cover: John Romita
Publisher: 1969 Marvel

Issue #1:

  • It's Only Magic!, script by Stan Lee, pencils by John Buscema, inks by John Verpoorten. Tommy steals a spellbook and summons a Djinn who plans to destroy the world.
  • Mr. Craven Buys His Scream House!, script by Denny O'Neil, art by Tom Sutton. Creighton Craven tries to destroy his grandfather's house.
  • Always Leave 'Em Laughing!, script by Gary Friedrich, pencils by Don Heck, inks by Frank Giacoia. Boswell Boliver builds a time machine to escape into the past where he believes that people won't laugh at him.
