Bob Marley, Tale of the Tough Gong


Series: 2 issue mini series
Illustrator: Gene Colon, Tennyson Smith (cover)
Writer: Charles E. Hall
Publisher: 1994 Marvel Music

Foreword by U2's Bono. Beginning the life story of the reggae superstar who grew out of poverty to change the world.

BOOK ONE: IRON follows Bob's early days in Jamaica and the formation of his band, the Wailers.

BOOK TWO: LION. Bob Marley rises to international fame with big hits like "I Shot the Sheriff" and "Stir It Up!" While back in Jamaica, several unscrupulous mystery men conspire to kill the reggae superstar.
