Basement Comics is an independent comic book publisher owned by Budd Root. Basement Comics is best known for publishing the popular good-girl title Cavewoman (and all its associated specials, annuals, and one-shots.) A division of Amryl Entertainment, Basement Comics is based in Mount Airy, North Carolina, and was founded in 1993. http://www.amryl.com/index.php
