Halo Escalation

Series: 24 issues, 2013-2015
Publisher: Dark Horse
Cover by Michael Atiyeh, Anthony Palumbo
Written by Chris Schlerf
Illustrated by Sergio Arino, Juan Castro

Halo, the most celebrated video game series of all time, joins forces with Dark Horse for a thrilling new ongoing series! The UNSC flagship Infinity is assigned escort duty for a diplomatic mission between the Arbiter and a powerful chieftain of the Brute tribes-but when a rogue Covenant group attacks, it's going to take all the firepower the Spartans have to keep the delegates alive!
