Elektra & Wolverine The Redeemer


Series: 3 issue mini series, 1 collection, 2002
Publisher: Marvel

Collects Elektra and Wolverine The Redeemer #1-3. Sophisticated prose by Greg Rucka and gorgeous illustrations by Yoshitaka Amano combine to make a modern masterpiece! Greg Rucka, famed crime novelist and comics scribe, brings two of Marvel's enduring icons together in a tale of mysterious pasts and uncertain futures!

When Wolverine is assigned to protect the same individual Elektra is hired to kill, sparks fly! Featuring the painted art of Japan's premiere illustrator, Yoshitaka Amano, this handsome deluxe hardcover will make a great edition to any collector's bookshelf!
