Elektra Lives Again


Series: one-off, 1990
Publisher: Marvel

Written by Frank Miller. Art by Frank Miller and Lynn Varley. Guest-starring Matt Murdock (Daredevil). The blind lawyer Matt Murdock's heightened senses detect a familiar scent comes on the wind. Can it be? Has his murdered love Elektra returned from the dead? But what do the assassins known as the Hand want with her and what new evil now arises in her wake.
