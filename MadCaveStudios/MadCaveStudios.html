Mad Cave Studios is an independent comic book publisher based out of Miami, Florida that was established in 2014 by Mark London. Mad Cave has published a range of comic books and trade paperbacks which are being distributed by Diamond Comic Distributors.

Titles

  • Battlecats (2016)
  • Hellfighter Quin (2020)
  • Honor and Curse (2019)
  • Knights of the Golden Sun (2018)
  • Over the Ropes (2019)
  • Savage Bastards (2019)
