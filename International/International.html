Series: 5 issues 1947, title change to International Crime Patrol for 1 issue 1948, then Crime Patrol for 10 issues 1948 - 1950, then Crypt of Terror 3 issues 1950, then Tales from the Crypt for 26 issues.
Publisher: EC

Gardner Fox wrote the stories for this E.C. title which began a morphing sequence which ended as The Crypt of Terror. The Pre-Trend title began as a crime comic book and it had adventures of continuing characters Van Manhattan and Madelon. The first morphing occurred with #6, which was retitled International Crime Patrol, introduced in #5 with the cover copy “Introducing the INTERNATIONAL CRIME-BUSTING PATROL…featuring Van Manhattan, Igor, Madelon and the Chessmen…in a thrilling book-length story!”
