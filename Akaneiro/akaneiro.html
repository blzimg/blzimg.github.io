Akaneiro is the reimagining of Red Riding Hood as a monster hunter in mythological Japan. Akaneiro is the Japanese word for the color of dye from the Madder plant, a dark shade of red. The comic series is based on the game by American McGeeâ€™s Spicy Horse Games, which reenvisions Red Riding Hood set in the wondrous world of Japanese folklore.

Issue #1: Kani is a young woman torn between two cultures on an epic adventure battling devious yokai for the fate of her world.

Issue #2: The brave young warrior Kani battles a gauntlet of fantastic mythical beasts inspired by Japanese folklore. However, things are not what they seem, and danger lurks where Kani least expects it.

Issue #3: Kani, a brave but inexperienced young huntress, uncovers a cunning plot by the demonic yokai, forcing her to make a decision that will shape her destiny and that of her entire world.
