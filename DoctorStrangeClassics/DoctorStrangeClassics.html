#1 New pin-ups by Kevin Nowlan, Jon Jay Muth, and Carl Potts. All of the stories are Stan Lee/Steve Ditko reprints: "The Defeat of Dr. Strange" (from Strange Tales 130), "The Hunter and the Hunted!" (ST 131), and "Face-To-Face At Last With Baron Mordo!" (ST 132). New Al Milgrom/John Byrne wraparound cover.

#2 New pin-ups by Tony Salmons, Al Milgrom, and Dan Green. All of the stories are Stan Lee/Steve Ditko reprints: "A Nameless Land, a Timeless Time!" (from Strange Tales 133), "Earth Be My Battleground" (ST 134), and "Eternity Beckons!" (ST 135). New John Byrne/Milgrom wraparound cover.

#3 New pin-ups by Arthur Adams, Craig Russell, and Bill Sienkiewicz. All of the stories are Stan Lee/Steve Ditko reprints: "What Lurks Beneath the Mask?" (from Strange Tales 136), "When Meet the Mystic Minds!" (ST 137), and "If Eternity Should Fail!" (ST 138). New John Byrne/Al Milgrom wraparound cover.

#4 Final issue of the series. New pin-ups by Arthur Adams, Michael Golden, and Steve Leialoha. All of the stories are Stan Lee/Steve Ditko reprints: "Beware! Dormammu is Watching!" (from Strange Tales 139), "The Pincers of Power!" (ST 140), and "Let There Be Victory!" (ST 141). New John Byrne/Al Milgrom wraparound cover.
