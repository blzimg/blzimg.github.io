Bloodseed


Series: two issue mini series
Illustrator: Liam Sharp, Cam Smith
Writer: Paul Neary
Publisher: 1993 Marvel

This offering from Marvel UK's Frontier line features a cover enhanced with gold ink. Bloodseed is programmed to deliver a genetic communication housed in his body. To do it, he'll have to fight his way through a savage land populated with every kind of deviant known to humanity.
