Series: 3 issues 1997
Written by Tom Brevoort, Mike Kanterovich
Illustrated by Art by Scott Kolins , John Lowe
Cover by Scott Kolins
Publisher: Marvel

An all-new saga of the early days of the legendary Captain Marvel. A famous Kree military hero has disappeared into the deadly Brood galaxy, and leading the mission to rescue him is a young, idealistic soldier named Mar-Vell. Others have designs on the missing Kree, namely the Skrulls and the Shi'ar. Plus, an early appearance of the Silver Surfer.
