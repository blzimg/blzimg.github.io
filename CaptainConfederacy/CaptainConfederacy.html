Captain Confederacy


Series: on-going
Publisher: Epic (Marvel)

Captain Confederacy and Kid Dixie head for a meeting of the world's heroes in a world where the South won its independence from the Union. Someone doesn't want them to arrive alive. Framed for the murder of a fellow super hero, Captain Confederacy and Kid Dixie search New Orleans for the real killer. Captain Confederacy and Kid Dixie finally discover the secret agenda for the Super Hero conference, but now their whole world is in danger!

Written by Will Shetterly, with art and cover by Vince Stone.
