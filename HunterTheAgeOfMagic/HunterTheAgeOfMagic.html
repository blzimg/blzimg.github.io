Hunter the Age of Magic

Series: 25 issues 2001-2003
Publisher: Vertigo (DC)
WRITTEN BY DYLAN HORROCKS
Illustrated by RICHARD CASE
Painted Cover by John Bolton

Tim Hunter from Books of Magic returns! Older. Wiser. More powerful. But still without all the answers. HUNTER begins an all-new ongoing series where Tim's adventures bring him to realms both familiar, Gemworld and Skartaris, and unknown, aided by his owl familiar Yo-Yo.
