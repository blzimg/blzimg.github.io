Elektra Saga


Series: 4 issue mini-series reprint, 1984
Publisher: Marvel

Elektra: The Complete Saga: Part 1 of 4 - "The Wall!" Written by Frank Miller. Art by Frank Miller and Klaus Janson. Includes material from Daredevil (1st Series) # 168 and #190 and Bizarre Adventures (1981 Magazine) #28. Relive the full history of the mysterious super ninja known as Elektra. What is her past connection to the masked hero Daredevil and how did she become the Kingpin's deadliest assassin?
