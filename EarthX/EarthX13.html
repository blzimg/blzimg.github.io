EarthX


It's the double-sized finale to the acclaimed saga!
The entire EARTH X cast takes on the Celestials in an epic showdown for the fate of the world! They're all here: Reed Richards! Captain America! Daredevil! The Asgardians! The Atlanteans! X-51! The all-new X-Men! Spider-Man! Colossus! Man-Wolf! The Inhumans! Bruce Banner and The Hulk! Loki! Namor! And, the amazing return of Mar-Vell!

And, not only does this brutal battle end the story known as EARTH X, but it also plants the seeds for the upcoming series that will be called UNIVERSE X!

Plus: The painted cover by Alex Ross is the final part of the 14-piece interconnecting portrait of the Earth X Universe!
