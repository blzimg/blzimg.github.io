Domino


Series: 3 issue mini-series 1997, 4 issue mini-series 2003.
Publisher: Marvel

1997: We know she's a longtime "partner" of Cable. We also know she currently co-leads the mutant strike force called X-Force. But what do we really know about Domino? Now all the pieces fall into place in this 3-issue limited series! Written by Ben Raab. Art and cover by David Perrin and Harry Candelario.

2003: Writer: Joe Pruett. Illustrator: Brian Stelfreeze. The fan-favorite X-Men character explodes in her long-awaited solo limited series. Her past has always been a mystery, even to her, but soon Domino will discover secrets about herself than even she would rather have never uncovered.
