4 issues 1984 Marvel
Illustrator: Don Perlin, Bill Sienkiewicz (cover)
Writer: Ann Nocenti

Story starts in Marvel Graphic Novel #12 "Dazzler: The Movie", where she was outed as a mutant.

Beauty and the Beast Part 1:Beast is on vacation from the Defenders and heads to Hollywood to hang out with old Avenger's pal, Wonder Man.

Dazzler, whose singing career was ruined when the world learned she was a mutant, is attending a party when she is approached by Alexander Flynn who wants her to perform at an experimental underground theater. Tired of being out of the limelight, she agrees and signs a contract with Hugo Longride.

Hank and Simon run into Dazzler at another party and are disgusted by the sleazy new company she is keeping. Dazzler's powers begin to erupt out of control and she abruptly disappears. Beast tracks her down at the Heartbreak Hotel and promises to take care of her in her time of need.
