Doctor Strange vs. Dracula


Series: one-shot, 1994
Publisher: Marvel

This 48-page horror fest pits the master of the mystic arts versus Dracula! Don't miss the epic reprinted from Tomb of Dracula (1972 1st Series) #44 and Doctor Strange (1974 2nd Series) #14. A special bonus: a story from Vampire Tales (1973) #9. Written by Marv Wolfman, Steve Englehart and Doug Moench. Art by Gene Colan, Tom Palmer and Russ Heath. Cover by Kyle Baker.
