Hell's Angel

Series: 5 issue mini-series 1992
Publisher: Marvel UK

Cover art by Liam Sharp. Hell's Angel, script by Bernie Jaye, pencils by Geoff Senior, inks by Cam Smith; the origin of Hell's Angel; The X-Men guest-star. Bullpen Bulletins; Stan's Soapbox; Stan Lee column about how proud he is of the current batch of Marvel creators.
