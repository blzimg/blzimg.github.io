In "The Commune," Syzygy, Oedi and Skeevo visit the Commune to do some business and come face to face with the assassin Tuetun. And it turns into a battle of wits between Tuetun and Oedi, of which, in Oedi's words: "I doubt ol' two ton has the ammunition for such an encounter." Inside back cover has a comedic photo feature with Jim Starlin. Back cover is a full color pinup of Skeevo. Script by Jim Starlin and art by Starlin and Al Milgrom.
