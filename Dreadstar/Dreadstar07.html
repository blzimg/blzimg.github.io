When Dreadstar goes alone to try discover one of the Instrumentality's most closely guarded secrets, the Lord High Papal has a deadly trap waiting for him in "Mindtrap." Script by Jim Starlin and art by Starlin and Josef Rubinstein. Issue also has The Interstellar Toybox in "Devolutionary-Dilemma" by Berni Wrightson. Inside back cover is a "How to Write and Draw Comics Good" comedic photo feature. Back cover is a full color pinup of Willow.
