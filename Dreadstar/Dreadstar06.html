Dreadstar and his team unleash Plan M which includes a mysterious leader known only as Maxilon in "Siege." Script by Jim Starlin and art by Starlin and Josef Rubinstein. Issue also has The Interstellar Toybox in "One-War Over Easy" by Berni Wrightson. Inside back cover is a "How to Write and Draw Comics Good" comedic photo feature. Back cover is a full color Wrightson pinup.
