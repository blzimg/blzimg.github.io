1975 Series:

While hunting, an adult Tor remembers a valuable lesson he learned as a child. Tor is banished from his tribe. Tor rescues villagers from the Fire-Men. Tor brings together a male-only tribe and a female-only tribe. A crippled tribe member enjoys killing every animal he can find. Tor helps a tribe fight off giants. Tor helps a tribe that lives underground. Tor witnesses a battle between two dinosaurs. Tor battles a giant turtle and escapes from a forest fire.

1993 Series:

When Tor comes to the rescue of a woman about to be sacrificed by a strange tribe, he is taken prisoner. Tor's captors subject him to a battery of painful tests, promising to admit him into their tribe, if he survives. After standing trial, Tor is subject to many hardships, culminating in an all out battle with the repulsive Lizard King. Tor returns to his home tribe to avenge the death of his father.
