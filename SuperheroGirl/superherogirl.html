Superhero Girl


Series: on-shot humor
Illustrator: Faith Erin Hicks
Writer: Faith Erin Hicks
Publisher: 2013 Dark Horse

About: What if you can leap tall buildings and defeat alien monsters with your bare hands, but you buy your capes at secondhand stores and have a weakness for kittens?

Cartoonist Faith Erin Hicks brings charming humor to the trials and tribulations of a young, female superhero, battling monsters both supernatural and mundane in an all-too-ordinary world. A lighthearted twist on the superhero genre.
