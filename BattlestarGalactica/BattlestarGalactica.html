Battlestar Galactica


Series: on-going, 23 issues
Illustrator: Ernie Colon, Dave Cockrum (cover)
Writer: Glen Larson, Roger McKenzie
Publisher: 1979-1981 Marvel

In a distant time and place, a terrible war was waged between man and machine... the humans lost. Now, guided by their last surviving warship,the mighty Battlestar Galactica, a handful of survivors travel through space in search of the lost home of their ancestors, a shining planet known through ancient records, a planet called Earth.

The last survivors of the human race are on a quest across the galaxy in search of their ancestral home, a planet called Earth. But a race of machines, the Cylons, is hunting them down and will not stop until every last human has been exterminated!
