50 issues 2005 Marvel
Illustrator: Mark Brooks, Cover: Rob Liefeld

Wade Wilson and Nathan Summers are back, and this time, they're stuck with each other! Can two grown men with deadly genetic weaponry work together without driving each other crazy?!

Action, adventure, black-ops, black humor and lots and lots of gunfire mark the return of your two favorite mutant anti-heroes to their own ongoing series!
