Force Works


Series: 22 issues 1994-1996
Publisher: Marvel

"Day-Break" Part 1 of 3. Guest-starring the Vision and Black Widow. Script by Dan Abnett and Andy Lanning. Pencils by Tom Tenney. Inks by Rey Garcia. Foldout popup wraparound cover by Tom Tenney and Rey Garcia.

Following the breakup of the West Coast Avengers, Tony Stark decides to form his own team in Southern California and recruits his former teammates to fill the ranks. Rogue Kree agents seek vengeance on Wonder Man and the Vision as a result of Operation Galactic Storm. When the Scarlet Witch and Wonder Man bump into the Vision and Black Widow at their former west coast compound, the Kree terrorists attack en masse.

The U.S. Agent and Shell-head lead the charge at Palos Verdes, while Wonder Man soars into the stratosphere to knock out the Kree's ion cannon. Something goes horribly wrong in outer space, and the new team cannot celebrate their victory in California, even less so when the Scatter appear! Who are these bizarre aliens?

Force Works Lineup: Iron Man, Scarlet Witch, Spider-Woman (aka Julia Carpenter), Wonder Man, and the U.S. Agent.
