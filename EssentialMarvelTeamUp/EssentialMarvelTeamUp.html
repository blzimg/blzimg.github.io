Essential Marvel Team Up


Series: 4 issue reprints
Publisher: Marvel

Collects Marvel Team-Up (1972-1985 1st Series) #1-24. Written by ROY THOMAS, GERRY CONWAY and LEN WEIN. Art by GIL KANE, JIM MOONEY and ROSS ANDRU. Cover by JOHN ROMITA SR. The wisecracking web-slinger Spider-Man teams up with the biggest stars of the Marvel Universe in these classic adventures - including the Incredible Hulk, the Uncanny X-Men, Ghost Rider, the Fantastic Four and the Avengers!
