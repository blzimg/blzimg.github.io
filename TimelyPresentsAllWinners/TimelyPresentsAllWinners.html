Series: Graphic Novel 1999
Cover by Ray Lago
Publisher: Marvel

In the tradition and format of the recent TIMELY PRESENTS: HUMAN TORCH COMICS comes the very first appearance of Marvel's first ever super-team, the All-Winners Squad!

Captain America and Bucky, the Human Torch and Toro, the Whizzer, Miss America and the Sub-Mariner gather to combat Isbisa, the criminal mastermind of the ages! But has Namor betrayed the team and joined the bad guys?! This action-packed story originally ran in ALL-WINNERS #19 way back in 1946.
