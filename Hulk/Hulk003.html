The Incredible Hulk


Illustration by Jack Kirby, Dick Ayers
Script by Stan Lee

"Banished to Outer Space!"
The military captures Rick and Ross convinces him that they need the Hulk to test a new experimental rocket for national security. Rick tricks the jade giant into the capsule but once it is shot into space, he realizes that Ross deceived him to get rid of his foe.

"The Ringmaster"
Rick takes a break from his Hulk-sitting duties and goes to the circus.
