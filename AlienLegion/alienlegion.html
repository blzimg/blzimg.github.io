Alien Legion



Series: on-going, 38 issues. 1 Graphic Novel, 1 Omnibus Dark Horse
Illustrator: Frank Cirocco, Terry Austin
Writer: Carl Potts, Alan Zelenetz
Publisher: 1983-1990 Epic (Marvel); 2009 Dark Horse

About: Created by Carl Potts, Alan Zelenetz, and Frank Cirocco for Marvel Comics's Epic Comics imprint in 1983. It features a military unit, Force Nomad, similar to the French Foreign Legion.

The 18-issue Alien Legion in 1987-1990, minus "The", was generally scripted by Chuck Dixon and penciled by Larry Stroman.

Issues:

  • The Alien Legion #1-20. 1983-1987 Epic (Marvel)
  • The Alien Legion Graphic Novel. 1986 Epic (Marvel)
  • Alien Legion #1-18. Epic (Marvel)
  • Alien Legion: Slaughterworld (1991 Marvel), collects The Alien Legion #1 and 7-11.
  • Force Nomad and Piecemaker, collecting the second series. Checker Book
  • Footsloggers collects the first six issues of the first volume. Checker Book
  • On the Edge and Tenants of Hell mini series reprint. Titan Books
  • Alien Legion Omnibus Volume 1 collecting the first seven story arcs of the original Epic Comics series. Dark Horse
  • Alien Legion Omnibus Volume 2 rounding out the first series including the graphic novel. Dark Horse
