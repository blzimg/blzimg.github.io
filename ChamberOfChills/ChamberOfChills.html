Numbering continues from Blondie Comics Monthly #1-20, the horror anthology and title "Chamber of Chills" starts with issue #21, then reverts the issue numbers after issue #24 to #5, continues with a title change to Chamber of Clues #27, which is an unrelated detective series. Marvel picks the title up in 1972 for 25 issues until 1976.

Changing titles and numbering is confusing, but used to happen all the time. The publishers used to have to file for a new mail order permit when they changed the numbering, so instead of stopping one and starting another, they often just changed titles and kept the numbering the same.
