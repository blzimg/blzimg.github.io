Chamber Of Chills


Series: on-going
Publisher: 1951 Harvey, 1972 Marvel

A series of unrelated pre-code horror stories.
Numbering continues from Blondie Comics Monthly #1-20, starting with issue #21, starts renumbering after issue #24 to #5, continues with a title change to Chamber of Clues #27, which is a detective series. Marvel picks the title up in 1972 for 25 issues until 1976.
