Comet Man


Illustrator: Bill Sienkiewicz
Writers: Bill Mumy, Miguel Ferrer
Series: 6 issue mini-series
Publisher: 1987 Marvel

Comet Man first appeared in Comet Man #1 (February 1987), and appeared in the rest of the series from Comet Man #2-6 (March–July 1987). The character subsequently appears in Fantastic Four #315-317 (June–August 1988), Marvel Comics Presents #50-53 (May–July 1990), and Captain Marvel vol. 5 #7 (July 2000).

Stephen Beckley pilots a craft through a tail of a comet and meets Max, a being from the Colony Fortisque. Max claims his race is responsible for starting evolution on Earth and other planets in the Milky Way Galaxy. Max had accidentally vaporized Beckley when they meet. He uses his technology to recreate Beckley, which grants him various superpowers.
