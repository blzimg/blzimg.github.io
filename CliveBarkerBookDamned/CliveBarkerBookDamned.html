Clive Barker's Book of the Damned


Series: 4 issue mini series
Publisher: 1991 Epic (Marvel)

A Hellraiser Companion. A notebook of research into the rumored existence of the Cenobites, as one man is inexorably pulled into Hell's tangled web. Take a look into the secret journal of Philip LeMarchand, the mad genius architect of those hellish puzzle boxes!

Written by Larry Wachowski, with art by Andrew Paquette, John Bolton, John Rheaume, Eliot Brown, John Van Fleet, Jon J. Muth, Joe Barruso, Mark Evans, Daniel Brereton and Miran Kim. Cover by Simon Bisley.
