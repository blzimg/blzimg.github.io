Series: 1905 - 1906
Created by James "Jimmy" Swinerton
Publisher: King Features

Sam and His Laugh is an infectious series featuring a job-seeking black man who gleefully laughs at the hypocrisy and pomp of society. While Sam is drawn in the typical black man stereotype common in early 20th century pop culture, it’s clear that Swinnerton sides with him as an instinctive hero of disruption who tears down the walls of social order with a bellylaugh – exactly what humor comics are all about.

Sam usually starts each strip with his customary statement: "I seen yo' ad in dep paper."
