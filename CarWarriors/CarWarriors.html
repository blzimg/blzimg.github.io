Hartoon attempts to recruit Chevy Vasquez to drive a load of Virunella from Fort Delorean in the Upper Peninsula of Michigan to Lansing.

Other Auto-duelers head to Fort Delorean seeking fame and fortune in the deadly 500 mile race to Lansing.

The race begins but it won't be easy if the road gangs have anything to say about it! Also, the most vicious and powerful road gang, The Vikings, gain an ally in the Soy Boy Corporation who want to keep their food supply monopoly at any cost.
