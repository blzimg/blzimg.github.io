8 issues in 2005 by IDW. At last, the entire GrimJack saga, in order, starting off with the classic stories from First Comics' Starslayer, issues 10 through 18. This initial volume concludes with the 28-page crossover with Mike Grell's famed swashbuckler, Starslayer. There is are also eight entirely new pages, plus unpublished art, bizarrely charming introductions, and a painted cover. This is where Cynosure begins!
