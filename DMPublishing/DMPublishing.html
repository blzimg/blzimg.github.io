This publisher primarily produced pulp magazines, first under the ownership of Fred Armer (1926-1932), then under the ownership of Harry and Irwin Donenfeld, with Armer as editor (1932-1950). The publisher names of record included variations on King, Merwil, D.M., Trojan, Culture, and Arrow.
