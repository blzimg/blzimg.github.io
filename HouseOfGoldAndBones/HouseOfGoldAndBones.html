House of Gold And Bones

Series: 4 issues and 1 collection (TPB) 2013
Publisher: Dark Horse
Written by Corey Taylor
Illustrations by Richard Clark, Dan Jackson and Jason Shawn Alexander

In a nightmarish world, the Human finds himself at a crossroads as wild adventure ensues and he's thrust into a morphing landscape of rolling hills, dense forest, and an ominous Crimson City. Chased by a crazed mob and mysterious characters neither friend nor foe, the Human takes an unexpected journey that will lead to his salvation... or damnation!
