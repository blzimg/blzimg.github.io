the Escapists


Series: 6 issue mini-series 2006, 1 collection TPB 2007
Publisher: Dark Horse

Collects Escapists (2006) #1-6. Written by Brian K. Vaughan. Art by Steve Rolsto, Philip Bond, Jason Shawn Alexander, and Eduardo Barreto.

Three aspiring comics creators with big dreams, small cash, and publishing rights to one forgotten Golden Age hero, The Escapist! Inspired by Michael Chabon's Pulitzer Prize-winning novel The Amazing Adventures of Kavalier and Clay, this is Vaughan's love letter to his chosen medium, a story about what it takes to start out with nothing in Cleveland, Ohio and end up with a comic so hot a major corporation wants to steal it from you!

Brilliantly interweaving the lives of the creators with the world of their creation, artists Steve Rolston and Philip Bond bring the comic-booking trio Maxwell Roth, Case Weaver, and Denny Jones to life, while the inimitable Jason Shawn Alexander and Eduardo Barreto illustrate the thrilling trials and exciting extrications of the new-and old-Escapist!
