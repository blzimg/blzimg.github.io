Holografix and embossed cover, art by by Tom Raney and Keith Williams. Things Past, script by Jim Starlin, pencils by Tom Raney, inks by Keith Williams; Infinity Crusade crossover; Darklore reviews the history of Adam Warlock and tries to take his Infinity Gem.

Chaos and Order monitor Adam Warlock's movements; Warlock remembers ignoring Eternity's arguments about his use of the Infinity Gauntlet; Warlock brings himself back to reality and meets with the Reality Gem's guardian, Thanos. Mephisto has made his proposition to Thanos and Warlock: he will provide information about the Cosmic Cubes and they will in turn give him one after the Goddess' defeat; Warlock refuses to give him such power and Mephisto challenges him; Warlock reminds him that he has bested him before in his own domain and says he will do no better in this realm.
