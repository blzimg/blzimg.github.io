Darkhawk


Cover: Mike Manley
Writer: Danny Fingeroth

Series: on-going, 50 issues

Dawn of the Darkhawk. Chris Powell finds the Darkhawk amulet; Bazin is unable to bribe Chris' mother (an assistant DA), but bribes Chris' father (a cop) who then leaves the family; Bazin and Hobgoblin want the Darkhawk power; Saint Johnny gives Chris the name Darkhawk.
