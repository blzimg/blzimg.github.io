Series: 8 issues
Publisher: Epic

Timespirits was an eight-issue comic book limited series published by Marvel Comics as part of its Epic Comics imprint in 1984. It was created by writer Stephen Perry and Tom Yeates. It is about the adventures of a time-traveling Native American Shaman and his Apprentice.

In a report published by Folha de S.Paulo, many have pointed to plagiarism of the comic committed by Avatar, the James Cameron movie. The stories are completely different, but there is a blue lady character that looks similar.
