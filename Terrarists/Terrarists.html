Series: 4 issues 1993 -1994
Publisher: Epic

Punisher 2099 writers Pat Mills and Tony Skinner's Heavy Hitter premieres! Fifteen years from now, according to this visionary comic, the Earth will be an ecological wasteland. It's up to four heroes, the Terrarists, to battle government corruption and corporate stooges from further plundering the planet! Written by Pat Mills and Tony Skinner. Art and cover by John Erasmus.
