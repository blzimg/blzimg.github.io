Silverline Comics. Steven Butler and Roland Mann worked as packagers for the Silverline Comics publisher before moving onto work with other publishers. In 1997, Roland Mann restarted a new Silverline Comics as publisher, which lasted until 2001. In 2019, Silverline restarted as a print on demand comic book publisher by Mann.
