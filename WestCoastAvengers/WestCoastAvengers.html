"Avengers Assemble!" Guest-starring the Shroud. Vision cameo. Story by Roger Stern. Art by Bob Hall and Brett Breeding. Earth's mightiest heroes are branching out. The Avengers have decided to form a west coast branch and Hawkeye has been charged with leading and forming the team. Who will make the cut?
