M.B. stood for the initials of George McManus and Rudolph Block, Jr., who founded the company with Alfred Block, Dr. William Rodgers and Frank J. Rice at 1493 Broadway, New York, NY. The company went bankrupt in late 1922.

Titles

  • Bughouse Fables (1921)
  • Comic Monthly (1922)
  • Katzenjammer Kids (1921)
  • Polly And Her Pals (1922)
  • Trouble of Bringing Up Father (1921)
