Blaze Carson, Rex Hart, Whip Wilson, Gunhawk

Series: started as Blaze Carson #1-5 (1948-1949), Rex Hart #6-8 (1949-1950), Whip Wilson #9-11 (1950), Gunhawk #12-18 (1950-1951)
Publisher: USA Comic Magazine, Marvel

Collections of western based short stories. The main character changed but the theme and format and numbering did not.
