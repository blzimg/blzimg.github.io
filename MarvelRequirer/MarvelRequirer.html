Marvel Requirer

Series: 34 issue free checklist insert 1990-1993
Publisher: Marvel

A free Marvel supplement checklist previewing / advertising upcoming comics. Also intended to be a satire of the National Enquirer with tabloid headlines like "My Hubby - The Human Spider". The Official Marvel Collectors' Checklist. Spotlighted releases for March 1990 include The Elsewhere Prince, X-Men Spotlight: The Starjammers, Ghost Rider #1, Hero #1 and the Rio Rides Again graphic novel.
