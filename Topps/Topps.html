Topps Company (http://www.topps.com) was established in 1938 with the transformation of a Brooklyn-based, family-owned tobacco-distribution business (American Leaf Tobacco) into the famous gum, confectionery and sports card company. From 1947 the interior of its gum wrappers often featured comics, including Bazooka Joe from 1953. The first Topps cards were free with packs of gum in 1949, with baseball cards appearing from 1952, followed by pop culture and comics-related cards starting in 1967. Topps Comics was a division of Topps Company, Inc. that published comic books from 1993 to 1998, beginning its existence during a short comics-industry boom that attracted many investors and new companies.
