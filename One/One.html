Series: 1 issue 1977
Publisher: PC

One is a one-shot 1977 comic published by PC, unrelated to the Rick Veitch series, but important for being the first comic published by Pacific Comics. It is a sci-fi anthology in magazine format, cover by Phillip Garris. Stories include:

  • "One" (by Stephen Garris and Nathan Carter Kingsbury)
  • "Hydra Celestial Battle" (script by Lana Evans, art by Lionel Sanders)
  • "Operation Changeling" (script by William R. Lund, pencils by David C. Weiss)
