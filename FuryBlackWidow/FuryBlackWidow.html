Fury Black Widow: Death Duty

Series: Graphic Novel 1995
Publisher: Marvel
Written by Cefn Ridout
Illustrations by Charlie Adlard

When a murder victim is found on the steps of the Russian Embassy Nick Fury, S.H.I.E.L.D., and the Black Widow are drawn into an intricate mystery involving Night Raven, the immortal gunman with a vendetta against crime.
