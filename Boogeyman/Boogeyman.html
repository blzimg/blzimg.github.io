4 issues in 1998 and 1 TPB collection in 1999, created by Sergio Aragones and published by Dark Horse.

Mark Evanier and together Mark and Sergio both pay tribute to the EC line of horror comics of the early 1950's like the Vault Of Horror. The Boogeyman has tales of people that happen into bad surroundings. A host with a banjo (like the Crypt Keeper) introduces the stories and he shows up once again in the last panel to send you back home. The drawings are crazy and wonderful and Sergio has panels full from edge to edge with his art. The stories tend to be moral tales of good and evil and if you dig deep you can see the messages of these tales.
