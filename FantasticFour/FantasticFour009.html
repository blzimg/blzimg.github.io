Fantastic Four


Pencils by Jack Kirby, inks by Dick Ayers, script by Stan Lee.

"The End of the Fantastic Four!" The Fantastic Four are bankrupt and the Sub-Mariner is scheming to use this.

"Sub-Mariner Gives the Orders!" The Sub-Mariner offers the Fantastic Four one million dollars if they star in a film that he's producing.

"The Fury of Mr. Fantastic" Mr. Fantastic discovers that the film project is a setup.

"The Flame of Battle" Human Torch and Thing discover that the film project is a setup.

"Vengeance Is Ours!" The film is produced and an unwilling Sub-Mariner pays the Fantastic Four their money.
