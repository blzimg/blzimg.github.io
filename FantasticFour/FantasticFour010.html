Fantastic Four


Pencils by Jack Kirby, inks by Dick Ayers, script by Stan Lee.

"The Return of Doctor Doom!" Doctor Doom returns from his unwanted journey in space and visits Jack Kirby and Stan Lee in order to make them set a trap for the Fantastic Four.

"Back from the Dead!" Doctor Doom tells how he managed to return to Earth with the help of the Ovoids and then he changes bodies with Mr. Fantastic using Ovoid technology.

"The End of Mr. Fantastic?" Doctor Doom convinces the rest of the Fantastic Four that he is Mr Fantastic.

"No Place to Turn!" Doctor Doom (as Mr. Fantastic) displays his new shrinking-ray and Mr. Fantastic (in Doom's body) escapes his captivity in Doom's castle.

"The Real Doctor Doom!" Doctor Doom and Mr. Fantastic get their bodies back and Doom is put under his own shrinking-ray and shrinks down to sub-atomic size.

Cameos by the Mole Man, the Miracle Man, the Skrulls, the Sub-Mariner, the Ant-Man, the Hulk, and Thor. The Invisible Girl pinup by Kirby and Ayers.
