Fantastic Four


Pencils by Jack Kirby. Inks by Sol Brodsky. Script by Stan Lee. First Silver Age appearance of the Sub-Mariner in "The Coming of... Sub-Mariner!"

"On the Trail of the Torch!" The Fantastic Four is searching for the runaway Human Torch.

"Enter the Sub-Mariner!" Fleeing the Thing, the runaway Human Torch finds the amnesiac Sub-Mariner.

"Let the World Beware!" The Human Torch drops the Sub-Mariner into the sea and he regains his memory.

"Sub-Mariner's Revenge!" The Sub-Mariner sends Giganto to smash New York.

"Return to the Deep!" The Thing walks into Giganto with a bomb and blows him up. The Human Torch sweeps the Sub-Mariner and Giganto away into the sea. The Sub-Mariner says "I'll be back!"
