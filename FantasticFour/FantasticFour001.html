Fantastic Four


Pencils by Jack Kirby. Inks by George Klein. Script by Stan Lee.

"The Fantastic Four!" Dr. Reed Richards takes his friends on a rocket trip that changes their lives forever.

"Meet the Mole Man!" Atomic plants disappear all around the world; Reed Richards pinpoints a place which is at the same distance from them all: Monster Isle.

"The Moleman's Secret!" Having captured the Fantastic Four, Moleman recounts how he discovered his underground kingdom.
