Fantastic Four


Pencils by Jack Kirby. Inks by Dick Ayers. Script by Stan Lee.

Part 1 "Captives of the Deadly Duo!" Doctor Doom presents himself to Sub-Mariner and offers him an alliance against the Fantastic Four and all mankind.

Part 2 "When Super-Menaces Unite" Dr. Doom gives a "Grabber" to Sub-Mariner and Human Torch discovers that Invisible Girl hides a photo of the Sub-Mariner in a book.

Part 3 "When Friends Fall Out!" The Sub-Mariner plants the "Grabber" in the Baxter Building and it is lifted into space by Doctor Doom.

Part 4 "Trapped!" The Baxter Building is set on a collision course with the Sun and the Sub-Mariner discovers that Doctor Doom has betrayed him.

Part 5 "The End... or the Beginning?" Doctor Doom is flung out into space and the Baxter Building is returned to its foundation. First mention of "unstable molecules."
