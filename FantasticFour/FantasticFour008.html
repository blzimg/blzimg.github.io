Fantastic Four

Pencils by Jack Kirby, inks by Dick Ayers, script by Stan Lee.

"Prisoners of the Puppet Master!" The Puppet Master tests his radioactive clay that gives him the power to remotely control people that are modeled in the clay.

"The Hands of the Puppet Maker" The Puppet Master gets the Thing under his command and imprisons the Invisible Girl. He then sends the Thing and Alicia Masters to infiltrate the Baxter Building.

"The Lady and the Monster!" While acting under the Puppet Master's influence, the Thing is subjected to one of Mister Fantastic's potions that changes him back to Ben Grimm.

"Face-to-face with the Puppet Master!" The Puppet Master stages a jailbreak just to prove his might.

"Death of a Puppet!" The jail break is stopped by the Fantastic Four and the Puppet Master falls to what seems to be his death.
