Series: 14 issues 1996 - 1997
Written by Ben Raab
Illustrated by Dave Hoover
Publisher: Marvel

Part of the short-lived all-ages .99 cent line of comics Marvel produced in the mid-'90's, Uncanny Origins focused on retelling the secret origins of it's characters.

Issue #1 "The Origin of Cyclops." Mr. Sinister, Alex Summers (Havok), Professor X, Jack O' Diamonds/The Living Diamond appearance.
