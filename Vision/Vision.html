Series: 4 issues 1994
Publisher: Marvel

"Dreams and Madmen" Part 1 of 4. Story by Bob Harras. Art by Manny Clark, Mike Machlan, and Al Vey. Cover by Steve Epting. The Vision is being haunted by dreams from his past, so he follows a mysterious beacon to New Orleans to find answers. Once there, the synthetic avenger encounters Ultron! Appearances by Deathcry, Giant-Man, and Jocasta.

"Impaired Vision" Part 2 of 4. Story by Bob Harras. Art by Manny Clark and Mike Machlan. Cover by Steve Epting. The Vision has been missing from the Avengers for almost a month. Strangely, the Vision and Jocasta have opened a detective agency in Chicago. Something bizarre is going on, and Crystal and Deathcry go to investigate.

"Visionary Dreams" Part 3 of 4. Story by Bob Harras. Art by Manny Clark and Mike Machlan. Cover by Steve Epting. A distressed Vision turns against his fellow Avengers and attacks Crystal and Giant-Man. What villain is causing the Vision's erratic behavior? And why has he captured Deathcry, Jocasta, and Ultron?

"Changing Visions" Part 4 of 4. Story by Bob Harras. Art by Manny Clark and Mike Machlan. Cover by Steve Epting. The epic conclusion to the Vision's limited series. The Vision regains his emotions and persona, but now he must defeat the evil Anti-Vision and save his friends! Appearances by Crystal, Deathcry, Giant-Man, and Laura Lipton.
