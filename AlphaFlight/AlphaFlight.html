Alpha Flight is a team of Canadian super heroes. The characters premiered in the April 1979 issue of X-Men. Marvel published an Alpha Flight comic book series from 1983 to 1994.

Issue collections:

  • Alpha Flight Classic vol2 Collects Alpha Flight #9-19 and X-Men #109
  • Alpha Flight Classic vol3 Collects Alpha Flight #20-29 and Incredible Hulk vol2 #313
  • X-Men: Alpha Flight Collects X-Men and Alpha Flight #1-2, X-Men/Alpha Flight (vol. 2) #1-2, X-Men #109, 120-121, 139-140
  • X-Men: Asgardian Wars Collects X-Men and Alpha Flight #1-2, New Mutants Special Edition #1, X-Men Annual #9
  • Alpha Flight vol1: You Gotta Be Kiddin' Me. Collects Alpha Flight (vol. 3) #1-6
