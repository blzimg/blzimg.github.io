Contest of Champions


Publisher: 1982, 1999, 2015 Marvel
Series: 3 issue limited series in 1982, collected as one-shot in 1999, back in 2015.

1982: "Chapter 1: A Gathering of Heroes!" Guest-starring every super hero in the Marvel Universe. Story by Mark Gruenwald, Bill Mantlo, and Steven Grant. Art by John Romita, Jr. and Pablo Marcos. Cover by John Romita, Jr. and Bob Layton.

The world's super heroes mysteriously disappear from Earth and then reappear in a giant arena in outer space. The Grandmaster and Death herself have decided to use the Earth's super heroes as pawns in a game they are playing against one another. If the Grandmaster wins the game, his brother, the Collector, will be restored to life. Both the Grandmaster and Death select 12 heroes to fight for their side and find the four pieces of the golden globe of life. Whichever team finds the most pieces wins!

Contest of Champions (1982) was the first Marvel Limited Series. The last two pages of this issue begin an index listing and description of each Marvel super hero.

2015: Written by Al Ewing. Art by Paco Medina and Thomas Labourot. Cover by Paco Medina. Welcome to the BATTLEREALM, a broken section of space-time where cosmic beings gamble for the ultimate power while their unwilling pawns fight for their lives and a chance to return back to Earth. Who will survive... THE CONTEST OF CHAMPIONS?
