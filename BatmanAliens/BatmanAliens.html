Batman Aliens


Series: 2 issue mini series
Illustrator: Bernie Wrightson
Writer: Ron Marz
Publisher: 1997 Dark Horse, DC

He's seen many faces of terror from his watch over Gotham City -- he's seen murderous clowns, strange creatures, and all forms of killer psychopaths. But he's never seen anything like this.

Amongst the Mayan ruins of the Amazon forest, the Dark Knight of Gotham is out of his element as he faces an intergalactic infestation of... Aliens.
