Fallen Angels


Series: 8 issue mini-series 1987, 1 Collection issue 2011
Publisher: Marvel

Written by Jo Duffy. Art by Kerry Gammill and Tom Palmer. Cover by Kerry Gammill and Tom Palmer.

From the pages of NEW MUTANTS: Sunspot and Warlock! From the pages of X-FORCE: the Vanisher and Boom-Boom! Mix these six unlikely allies with Devil Dinosaur, super-strong lobsters and some teenage runaways, and you've got the Fallen Angels! What wonders await these strange heroes of Beat Street in the paradise of Coconut Grove? Witness one of the strangest X-Men adventures of all time!
