Full Circle (sometimes spelled Cirkle) Press released a couple of titles, published between 2004-2008, showcasing the ability of Simon "Biz" Bisley to render not only stunning covers but also compelling, dynamic interiors. Pencils on Thicker Than Blood by Mike Ploog.

Titles

  • Full Cirkle (2004)
  • Full Cirkle II (2007)
  • Springheeled Jack (2006)
  • Thicker Than Blood (2007)
  • Whitechapel Freak (2004)
