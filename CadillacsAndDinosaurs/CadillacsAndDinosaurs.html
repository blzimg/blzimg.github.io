Just when the human race thinks it's in charge, Mother Earth sends earthquakes, tidal waves and volcanoes to push civilization back to the age of dinosaurs. But Jack Tenrec salvages a real prize - a classic Cadillac. Jack and the beautiful (and dangerous) Hannah Dundee begin their full color adventures in the future age of dinosaurs. Mark Schultz's fast and furious future fantasy originally appeared in black and white as Kitchen Sink's award-winning Xenozoic Tales.
