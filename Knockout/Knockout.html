Codename Knockout


Series: on-going, 23 issues
Publisher: 2001 Vertigo (DC)

Written by Robert Rodi. Art by Louis Small, Jr. and George Freeman. Cover by Joe Chiodo.
Vertigo's sexy spy thriller satire debuts with a special teaser #0 issue! Angela St. Grace and her sidekick Go-Go Fiasco become embroiled in their very first case in the lead story. Plus, a behind-the-scenes look at early design sketches by Louis Small, Jr. and commentary on the genesis of CODENAME: KNOCKOUT.
