Clive Barker's Nightbreed


Series: Graphic Novel
Publisher: 1991 Epic (Marvel)

Based on the story by Clive Barker. Adapted by Alan Grant and John Wagner. Art by Jim Baikie. Cover by Mike Mignola. A world of horrors beyond human ken, where the lost tribes of man are gathered, from the bizarre to the berzerker: Welcome to Midian, a world which the man who was Aaron Boone is destined to bring both destruction, and salvation. Welcome to the world of the NIGHTBREED.
