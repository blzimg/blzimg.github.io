Balder The Brave



Series: 4 issue mini series
Illustrator: Sal Buscema, Walter Simonson (cover)
Writer: Walter Simonson
Publisher: 1985 Marvel

Balder leaves Karnilla to join Thor in battle with Hel. Story continued in Thor #360-362.

Balder the Betrayed!, script by Walt Simonson, art by Sal Buscema; The Storm Giants capture Karnilla; Balder and Agnar follow them to Jotunheim, realm of the Giants.

Balder the Warrior!, script by Walt Simonson, art by Sal Buscema; Balder enter the Giants' castle, where they hold Karnilla captive.

Balder the Beautiful!, script by Walt Simonson, art by Sal Buscema; Balder rescues Karnilla and learns he has been chosen to replace Odin.
