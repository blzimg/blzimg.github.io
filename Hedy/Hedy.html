Hedy

Series: 28 issues #22-50 1947-1952
Publisher: Atlas (Marvel)

I have not found any Credits or story summaries, so there is nothing but a slideshow of covers, but Hedy is a comedy based Barbie character that has nothing to do with All Winners which is a super hero title featuring the All Winner squad starring Captain America, Sub Mariner and the Human Torch.

The title changes from Hedy De Vine (#22-35) to Hedy of Hollywood (#35-50) at issue #35 in 1950 to 1952.
