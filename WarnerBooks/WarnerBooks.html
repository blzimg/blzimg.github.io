Warner Books is now owned by Hachette Book Group (HBG), a publishing company owned by Hachette Livre, the largest publishing company in France. HBG was formed when Hachette Livre purchased the Time Warner Book Group from Time Warner on March 31, 2006. Hachette is considered one of the big-six publishing companies, along with Holtzbrinck/Macmillan, Penguin Random House, HarperCollins, Scholastic, and Simon & Schuster.
