Warner Books Website

Warner Books is now owned by Grand Central Publishing. Grand Central Publishing is a division of Hachette Book Group. Grand Central Publishing came into existence in March 2006 after Time Warner sold the Time Warner Book Group to Hachette Livre. They are considered one of the big-six publishing companies, along with Harper Collins, Macmillan, Penguin Random House, Scholastic, and Simon & Schuster.
