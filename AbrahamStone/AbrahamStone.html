Abraham Stone


Series: 2 issue mini series
Issues: #1 Radix Malorum, # 2 The Revolution
Publisher: 1995 Epic/Marvel
Illustrators: Joe Kubert
Writers: Joe Kubert

About: Rural steadfast Abraham Stone seeks destiny and vengeance in early 20th century New York City with mobsters, Hollywood, Pancho Villa, and more. Based on actual events.

Story: Stone saves a rich lady from being robbed. She is the owner of a film studio, and she takes Stone to California to be her kept man and to work in silent films. He acts in a number of films but is unhappy in Hollywood. He goes to Mexico and ends up in Pancho Villa's army.

Villa recruits Stone to take part in his raid on the United States at Columbus New Mexico in 1918. Stone refuses to fight against his country and turns a machine gun on the Mexican army. Just as Villa is about ot shoot Stone, the American Calvary comes.
