Harvey

Series: 6 issues 1970
Publisher: Marvel
Illustrations by Stan Goldberg
Script by Stan Lee

Harvey takes Duke's advice for dealing with Mr. Snorkel, but Duke himself make out much worse when he tries the same. Harvey and Raven keep missing each other when trying to meet for a roller skating date, and by the time they finally meet they're too tired to skate. The story of how Harvey met the rest of the gang when he moved to Midville.
