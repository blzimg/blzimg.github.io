Cyberspace 3000


Series: 8 issue min-series
Publisher: 1993 Marvel

Marvel UK presents Cyberspace 3000, with a cover that explodes with glow in the dark detail. This spectacular science fiction saga charts the harrowing journey of a group of Earth's survivors in desperate search of a future. Guest starring Galactus. Written by Gary Russell. Art by Steve Tappin and Michael Eve. Cover by Liam Sharp and Andy Lanning.

Galactus sends Dark Angel to investigate Sol III. As the Trilexia discovers more secrets of the Portal, Shan O'Meara finds a disturbing link between him and Dark Angel.

The Silver Surfer guest stars. With Captain Cabre-Rios lost and Engineer O'Meara gone, it's up to Onanwro to prevent Galactus from destroying Sol III. Things only get worse when the Surfer shows up looking for a fight.

Dark Angel attempts to rejoin her timeline, but the Trilexia make one last effort to take over. Meanwhile, Captain Cabre-Rios is trapped in cyberspace facing the one foe she cannot conquer!
