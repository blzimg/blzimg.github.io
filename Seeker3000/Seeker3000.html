Series: 4 issues 1998
Publisher: Marvel

BOOK 1: A NEW BEGINNING. Marvel explores the mysteries of space in an all-new sci-fi adventure! 25 years after its maiden voyage, the Seeker 3000 continues to search for a habitable world for its crew.

Onboard, however, turmoil rules. Shaw has authorized the creation of clones from the stored DNA cargo. Meanwhile, the telepaths among the crew, led by the enigmatic Phaedra, have secluded themselves into a deserted part of the immense ship as a dangerous caste system emerges.

Written by Dan Abnett and Ian Edginton. Art by Andrew Currie and Art Nichols
