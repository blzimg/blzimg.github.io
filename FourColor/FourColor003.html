Four Color

Written and Illustrated by V. T. Hamlin

Reprinted from Alley Oop (NEA Service) daily newspaper strips from 1937 and 1938

The women are running Moo. A stranger, Eeny from Jerooly, ingratiates herself with Queen Umpa. She plots to depose Umpa and declares herself dictator of Moo. She enlists Oop as an assistant. Oola and Foozy escape Moo with a group of resisters.
