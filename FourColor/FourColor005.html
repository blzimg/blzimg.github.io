Four Color

Written and Illustrated by George Kerr

"RAGGEDY ANN AND ANDY" No code number. Copyright 1942 by Johnny Gruelle Company.

When items begin disappearing from the toy room, Raggedy Andy stays awake and finds that a Loonie is taking them. He and Raggedy Ann trail the Loonie to a mysterious tree which is the entrance to the land of the Loonies. They explore the strange land until they find the missing items.

Realizing that the Old Horse did not come back with them from Loonie Land, Raggedy Ann and Andy return to Loonie Land the next night. They are guided by an old Loonie to the castle of the Dreadful Dragon, who they befriend. The Dragon flies them around until they find the Old Horse. After being reunited, the group finds that the Loonies have been taken by a giant.
