The Prowler appeared in Marvel Comics for awhile and by 1994 appeared in his own title. Several characters (such as the anti-hero Hobart "Hobie" Brown and the career criminal Aaron Davis) have taken up the Prowler identity all while being an acquaintance to Spider-Man.

Hobie Brown has appeared in various animations and video games. Aaron Davis makes his live action debut in Spider-Man: Homecoming portrayed by Donald Glover.
