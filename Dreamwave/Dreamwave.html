Dreamwave Productions was a Canadian art design studio and comic book publisher founded by Brothers Pat Lee and Roger Lee in Toronto, Canada in 1996 as an imprint under Image Comics, publishing their first mini-series Darkminds. Pat maintained artistic control while Roger managed the business operations. Dreamwave split off from Image Comics in April 2002 and is best known for their multiple Transformers comic book series after acquired the license for the Transformers toyline from Hasbro in December 2001. The company shut down on January 4, 2005.
