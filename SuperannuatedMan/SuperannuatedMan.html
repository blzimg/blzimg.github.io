Humanity has been overthrown. Mutated animals run amuck in a post-apocalyptic world. Think you've seen this type of thing before? Well, not the way Ted McKeever tells it. Follow the last remaining human, HE, as he encounters every manner of bizarreness the devastated town of Blackwater has to offer, in this collection of McKeever's brooding, darkly humorous series, The Superannuated Man.

The future. Humans have become an endangered species.The location: the remains of a small seaside town overrun by mutated, advanced, anthropomorphic animals. The protagonist: a grumpy, possibly crazy old man at the end of his rope, trying to hold on.
