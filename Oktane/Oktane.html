Series: 4 issues 1995, 1 TPB 1997
Writer: Gerard Jones
Illustrator: Gene Ha, Andrew Pepoy
Publisher: Dark Horse

The red-hot artist who brought you Cyclops and Phoenix and the writer who brought you Magneto and the Magnetic Men now bring you -- the hero who offended everybody! He's Oktane, nine friggin' feet of muscle, bad taste, and auto parts, and he's rockin' his way through an America gone stupid!

The cult-fave, four-issue series of creator-owned chaos that took shots at white Americans, black Americans, Native Americans, naive Americans, and even America's most sacred institution -- Las Vegas!
