Vic and Blood The Chronicles of a Boy and his Dog

Series: 2 issues 1987, 1 Graphic Novel 1989
Publisher: Mad Dog Graphics, NBM
Story by Harlan Ellison
Illustrations by Richard Corben

Legendary Heavy Metal artist Corben adapts Ellisons award-winning, controversial short story A Boy and His Dog, which also inspired the 1970s cult-classic film starring Don Johnson. In a ruined future, Vic hunts for survival and women with the aid of his telepathic dog, Blood. When Vic is lured into a subterranean paradise that needs virile men, its bad news for the buddy team. Also featuring another Vic and Blood story, Eggsucker.

Vic learns the horrifying truth about the underground culture of survivors, and escape means making a terrible choice. Also featuring another story that continues Vic and Blood storyline beyond the short story and film.
