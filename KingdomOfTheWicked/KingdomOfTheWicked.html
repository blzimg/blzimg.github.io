Kingdom Of The Wicked

Series: Graphic Novel 2004
Publisher: Dark Horse
Written by Ian Edginton
Illustrations by D'Israeli

Popular childrens author Christopher Grahame finds himself trapped in a hellish world based on his childhood fictions. As Christopher struggles to understand the war launched by the Land Under the Bed, his wife and doctors investigate the cause of his blackouts and make a shocking discovery. Presented for the first time in full color, with a new prologue.
