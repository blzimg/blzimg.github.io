Long Bow was a young Blackfoot Indian who was alive during the days of colonial America. He was orphaned when his parents, Great Bow and Looking Glass, were killed by members of the Crow tribe. He then took up and had adventures with the kindly Trapper Jim. Created or Co-created by Robert Webb with covers by Maurice Whitman.
