Storyworlds is a new independent comics publisher. The comics industry faces declining growth and challenges to its monthly distribution and attention models. We are working with new, diverse creators on global stories told in our new 50-60 page Storyworlds Graphic Novella format. We are launching 4 new books in 2021. We are publishing stories with a broad appeal, outside of caped-superhero books. We like high-concept, subversive ideas with diverse, global stories that can engage a large audience.
