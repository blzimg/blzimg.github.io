Cosmic Powers Unlimited


Series: 5 issue mini series
Cover: Claudio Castellini
Publisher: 1995 Marvel

Starmasters/Cosmic Powers x-over, part 1/5. Silver Surfer, script by Ron Marz, pencils by Scott Benefiel and Steve Carr, inks by Jasen Rodriguez, Bob Almond, and Mike Miller.

Mistress Death decides she wants the Silver Surfer as a consort, and Thanos is only too happy to comply. Thanos uses the Reality Gem to bring Mar-Vell back to life. Silver Surfer pinup by John Buscema. Silver Surfer pinup by Michael Golden.
