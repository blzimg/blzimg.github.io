Series: 3 issues 1975
Publisher: Atlas
Cover by Al Milgrom and Dick Giordano
Illustrated by Al Milgrom and Jack Abel
Written by Michael Fleisher

In the far-off era of 2001 AD, a genetic experiment creates the humanoid plant Morlock. Used as an assassin, he rebels against his totalitarian masters when he discovers his true nature, becoming the worlds strangest superhero. Part of the short-lived Atlas superhero line from former Marvel publisher Martin Goodman.
