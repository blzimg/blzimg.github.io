The Bakers Meet Jingle Belle


Series: one-shot
Illustrator: Kyle Baker
Writer: Paul Dini
Publisher: 2006 Dark Horse

When two multi-Eisner-award-winning storytellers bring their funniest creations together, the result is nothing less than pure comics magic.

As the Baker parents try to frantically finish their holiday preparations, they find themselves in dire need of a baby-sitter.

Enter Jingle Belle, fresh from a Christmas Eve defection from her father, Santa, and looking for a place to hide out. Somewhat skeptical of Jingle's heritage, the Baker kids demand proof so she obliges with a whirlwind sleigh ride to the North Pole.

Meantime parents Kyle and Liz are dealing with the horrors of last-minute shopping and the now-stranded Santa is mistaken for a prowler as he searches suburbia for both his daughter and his missing sleigh.
