Black Goliath


Series: on-going, 5 issues
Illustrator: George Tuska, Ron Wilson (cover)
Writer: Tony Isabella
Publisher: 1976 Marvel

Black Goliath establishes himself in Los Angeles, leading a research team for Stark Industries. As Black Goliath he investigates a series of radium robberies and finds Atom-Smasher and his gang.

Guest appearance by Hank and Janet Pym, Tony Stark (Iron Man) and Luke Cage.

Black Goliath's first appearance is in Luke Cage, Power Man 24. The story here continues in issue 25.
