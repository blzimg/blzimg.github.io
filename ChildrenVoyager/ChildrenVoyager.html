#1 Shadows and Fog. An immortal Wayfarer scattered the planet with incarnations of himself, hoping for absolute understanding of human life when re-absorbing his "children". But approaching his last child, Sam Wantling, he meets resistance.

#4 The electrifying conclusion! Sam and the world's greatest witch struggle for their very souls. Surprises abound!
