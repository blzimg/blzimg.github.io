He's a little guy with a big head, whose infectious infantile behavior is restrained only by a size 3 straightjacket and four concrete walls. Yes, Gregory is a lunatic - and quite proud of it too! Learn about the institutionalized Gregory through his interactions with his guardian The Man (actually an orderly), the doctors, and his friend Herman Vermin, an unfortunate rat destined to be killed and reincarnated again and again as... a rat.
