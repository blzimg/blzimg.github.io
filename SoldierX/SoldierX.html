Series: 12 issues 2002 - 2003
Written by Darko Macan
Illustrations by Igo Kordey
Publisher: Marvel

What has Cable become after the events of CABLE #107? Find out in this jump-on issue! Bored stiff while covering a Sumo tournament, reporter Irene Merryweather, Cable's old flame, gets entangled with S.H.I.E.L.D. and delivers an important hint as to the future of the man formerly known as Cable.

What has happened to turn Cable into Soldier X? The answers are revealed here, as the one-man mutant army goes to Russia with no love. The final story of Soldier X begins when a young mutant is put on death row for killing her parents. What Nathan Summers discovers about the murder and the young mutant's fate will lead him to unravel a mystery involving the Weapon X Program and the strange disappearances of mutants all over the country.
