Stories by Bruce Jones. Art by Bruce Jones, Don Lomax, and John Bolton. Cover by John Bolton A horror anthology from the mind of writer Bruce Jones, featuring art by some of the top illustrators of the time. A woman encounters a monster in a well, in a story with art by John Bolton. Lovers scheme to eliminate their husbands in time-worn plot, only this time both the lovers are women. A lonely boy befriends the creature that lives in a lake. The Well; Nick of Time; The Secret Place.
