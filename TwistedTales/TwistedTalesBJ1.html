Stories, art and cover by Bruce Jones. Eclipse revives the acclaimed post-Code horror anthology from the mind of writer Bruce Jones, with Jones providing the art as well in this series. A lonely princess falls in love with a merman, but she doesn't know how to swim; Booba the Jungle Boy makes a monkey friend, in a spoof of Tarzan-style comics. The Princess and the Merman; Point of View; Stopped; Outside-In; Booba the Jungle Boy.
