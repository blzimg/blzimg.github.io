Stories by Bruce Jones. Art by Alfredo P. Alcala, Bret Blevins, Richard Corben, and Tim Conrad. Cover by Richard Corben. A horror anthology from the mind of writer Bruce Jones, featuring art by some of the top illustrators of the time. A collections agent tries to take advantage of a woman. Vacationers in Germany are trapped in a series of fairy tales. Four teenagers seek revenge on the kids who killed their friend. Infected; Out of His Depth; A Walk in the Woods; All Hallows
