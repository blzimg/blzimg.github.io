Stories by Bruce Jones, Charles Wagner, Jan Strnad and Dennis Etchison. Art by Mike Hoffman, Thom Enriquez, Val Mayerik, and Bill Wray. Painted cover by John Bolton. Eclipse picks up the acclaimed post-Code horror anthology, previously published by Pacific. Ghostly teens from the 1950s haunt a highway. A pet dog becomes a werewolf. Women-like things from beneath the sea marry the men of a small town and raise up monsters, in a story by horror legend Dennis Etchison. Warped Panels; Deadlights; Spade the Werewolf and Me; Wet Season.
