Stories by Bruce Jones. Art by Bret Blevins, Doug Wildey, Richard Corben, and Bill Wray. Cover by Richard Corben A horror anthology from the mind of writer Bruce Jones, featuring art by some of the top illustrators of the time. A boy finds a dinosaur egg and hatches a T-Rex. Two people experience whatever a screenwriter adds to a horror movie script. A man schemes to murder another for his wife and money. Me an Ol Rex; Off Key; With Honor; Sunken Chest.
