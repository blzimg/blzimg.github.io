Stories by Bruce Jones, David Carren and Rick Geary. Art by Attilio Micheluzzi, Bernie Wrightson, Gray Morrow, Bill Wray, and Rick Geary. Painted cover by John Pound. A horror anthology from the mind of writer Bruce Jones, featuring art by some of the top illustrators of the time. The newcomer in a Canadian village learns the truth about Sasquatch, in a story with art by Swamp Thing creator Bernie Wrightson. Two tales of ironic justice reveal why a bear suit makes a bad choice for crime. A time traveler wants to witness the Lizzie Borden murders, but gets a little too close, in a story by comics legend Gray Morrow.
