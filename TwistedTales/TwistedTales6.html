Stories by Bruce Jones. Art by Attilio Micheluzzi, John Bolton, John Totleben, and Mike Hoffman. Painted cover by John Bolton. A horror anthology from the mind of writer Bruce Jones, featuring art by some of the top illustrators of the time. An old man can control other people's lives in his dreams. A pair of ghosts re-enact their violent deaths each night. A man and his dog both undergo strange transformations in the moonlight, in a story by Swamp Thing artist John Totleben. You, Illusion; Evening Walk; Home Ties; Roomers.
