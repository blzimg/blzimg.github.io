Stories by Bruce Jones. Art by Ken Steacy, Mike Ploog, Rand Holmes, and Val Mayerik. Cover by Bernie Wrightson. A horror anthology from the mind of writer Bruce Jones, featuring art by some of the top illustrators of the time. A man accidentally kills the woman he loves. Soldiers are plagued by rats on the battlefield. A cab driver picks up a fare who wants to go fast, in a rare above-ground story by underground comix legend Rand Holmes. Over His Head; Nightwatch; Infant Terrible; Speed Demons.
