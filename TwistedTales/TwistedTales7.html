Stories by Bruce Jones. Art by John Bolton, Bill Wray, Ian Akin, Brian Garvey, and Tanino Liberatore. Painted cover by John Bolton. A horror anthology from the mind of writer Bruce Jones, featuring art by some of the top illustrators of the time. A lonely old woman murders visitors and keeps their severed heads for company. A gigolo encounters his client's murderous mutant son. The truth about the Abominable Snowman is too horrible to bear, in a story by Heavy Metal artist Tanino Liberatore. Hollys Hobby; Hooked!; Sasquatch; Shut-In.
