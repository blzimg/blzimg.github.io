Stories by Bruce Jones, Rick Geary and William F. Nolan. Art by Butch (Jackson) Guice, Rick Geary, Mike Hoffman, and Thom Enriquez. Painted cover by John Pound A horror anthology from the mind of writer Bruce Jones, featuring art by some of the top illustrators of the time. An abused boy has a mental connection with the monster in the sewer. An 18th-century man murders his wife and her sister with an axe, in a one-pager by Rick Geary. A man is trapped for eternity in a hellish party, in a story by sci-fi legend William F. Nolan. Way Down There Below in the Dark; First Impressions; An Unsettling Incident; The Party. Final issue under Pacific Comics.
