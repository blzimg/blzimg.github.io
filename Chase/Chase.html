Chase was created by Dan Curtis Johnson and J.H. Williams III, and her first appearance was in Batman (DC, 1940 series) #550. The original Chase series was reprinted in graphic novel form in December 2011.

Cameron Chase is a member of a government agency for superhuman activity. She seems to have the power to damp the abilities of other superhumans.
