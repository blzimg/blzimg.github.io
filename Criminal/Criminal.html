Criminal


Writer: Ed Brubaker
Illustrator: Sean Phillips
Series: on-going, 10 issues
Publisher: 2006 Icon (Marvel)

Criminal is an ongoing American creator-owned crime comic book series by writer Ed Brubaker and artist Sean Phillips, originally published by Marvel Comics Icon imprint, and later by Image Comics.

The first series began in October 2006, and ran for ten issues, which were published as two trade paperback editions, Coward (issues 1-5) and Lawless (issues 6-10), in 2007.

In Coward, pickpocket Leo Patterson gets involved in an armored car heist that is not what it seems.

In Lawless, AWOL soldier Tracy Lawless infiltrates his brother Ricky's former gang to find out who murdered Ricky.
