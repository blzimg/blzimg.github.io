Conan the Barbarian #3

Script: Roy Thomas
Pencils: Barry Windsor-Smith
Inks: Sal Buscema
Colors: Mimi Gold
Letters: Sam Rosen

Conan the Barbarian Vs. the Grim Grey God!
Conan converses with God Borri Borri, participates in a great and bloody battle, and kills Malachi. Adapted from the non-Conan story, "The Grey God Passes" by Robert E. Howard. Includes a map of the lands of Hyboria based on a map prepared by Robert E. Howard.
