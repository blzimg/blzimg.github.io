Dark Horse Book of the Dead


Series: one-shot Graphic Novel
Publisher: 2005 Dark Horse

By Mike Mignola, Evan Dorkin, Jill Thompson, Kelley Jones, Gary Gianni, Robert E. Howard, Todd Herman, David Crouse, Roger Lanridge, Eric Powell, Bob Fingerman, Pat McEown, Guy Davis, Jamie S. Rich, Paul Lee, Brian Horton, and Scott Allie. Cover by Gary Gianni.

Mike Mignola presents a Hellboy yarn combining Shakespeare and graverobbing in this followup to Dark Horse's Eisner-nominated Books of Hauntings and Witchcraft. Anthology of original material featuring the award-winning talents of Mike Mignola, Evan Dorkin, Jill Thompson, Eric Powell, and more! Features an original Mike Mignola Hellboy story.
