Domination Factor


Publisher: Marvel
Series: 1999 FF/Avengers cross title story arc. 4 issue mini-sub-series per title, 8 issues. Numbering starts with FF going back and fourth with Avengers.
1.1, 2.3, 3.5, 4.7 Fantastic Four
1.2, 2.4, 3.6, 4.8 Avengers

The appearance of twin colossal constructs known as the Harbingers of the Everlasting Winter signal the beginning of the end for humanity, unless the Earth's Mightiest Heroes and comicdom's First Family leap into the past and recover the key to the world's salvation! But can the Avengers and the FF triumph, even when they must return to the moment of their lowest ebb?!
